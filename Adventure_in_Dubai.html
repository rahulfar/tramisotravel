Adventure in Dubai

Dubai is one of the best countries to visit and is a beautiful country. But is also a good country to visit by the adventure lovers. Following are the adventures one can experience in Dubai:-

1. Sky Diving –

Skydiving is one of the most adventures sport to perform in Dubai, specially because of Dubai’s beautiful view. Skydiving center in Dubai offers tandem parachuting with photos & video included, plus gyrocopter tours. After more than 300 years, skydiving has become one of the most adventures and extreme sports. Thousands of people’s jumps are made every year and one of the most popular places to experience this adventures and thrilling sport is Dubai from many years. Skydiving in Dubai is growing in popularity year over year, and with world’s best facilities, stunning and beautiful skydive locations and expert instructors for help, Dubai has owned its place as one of the leading skydiving destinations in the world for the tourists. People also have the option of skydiving on the beautiful man made Palm islands. Dubai is one of the best destinations for skydiving and offers a wonderful experience. You can experience skydiving on places like Skydive Dubai, Inflight Dubai Indoor Skydiving, Skydive Dubai Desert Dropzone, Skyhub Paramotors in Dubai.

2. Quad Biking -

Quad Biking is an all-terrain vehicle (ATV), also named as quad bike, is a four wheel vehicle that travels with low-pressure in tires, including a seat which is straddled by the bike operator, the bike comes along with the handlebars for steering controls. Quad biking includes riding the bike on different terrains, in Dubai's vast desert, at different levels of speeds. Quad biking is a four-wheeled bike fun experience, usually it is in an outdoor setting. The thrill and adventure of riding a four wheeled massive bike on amazing terrains with no limits or restrictions is incomparable. The quad biking is a next level adventure quotient of Dubai’s desert safaris with a wild bike ride in the great and adventures desert. The quad biking in Dubai is one of the most popular and adventures experiences to witness in Dubai. You get the chance to pass through the desert with abandon and all alone, you can take any route you want to reach your destination and there is no one to stop you and you will just have an all round amazing time in this ride. Four-wheeler is one of the best and a fun way of getting out into the real life adventure, way far from traditional routes and straight roads. With proper safety measures taken by the biker, quad biking is one of the most fun experience to have in Dubai. If you enjoy off road driving and like motor sports, quad biking is a perfect match for you.

3. Skiing at ski Dubai -

For skiing in Dubai you don’t have any hill stations or real snow fall. Even after this you can experience skiing in Dubai, for skiing experience in Dubai you can always visit Ski Dubai, which is an indoor snow park of Dubai and is one of the largest snow park in the world. Its is a resort which is known as indoor sky resort which features 22,500 square meters of indoor ski area. The park maintains the temperature between -1 degree to 2 degrees Celsius throughout the year. It is a part of the Mall of the Emirates, one of the biggest shopping malls in the world with world class opulent brands from all over the world, located in Dubai. The very known Ski resort of Dubai features an 85-metre-high indoor mountain with 5 slopes with different steepness and difficulty, it also includes a 400-metre-long run, the world's first indoor black diamond run, and various features which are changed on a regular basis. A quad lift and a tow lift carry skiers and snowboarders up the mountain like the one in real hill stations. Equipment such as skis and jackets are provided with the ticket and one can also buy equipment in the nearby stores. Ski Dubai has 3000 square meter snow park to play for the visitors, the area also comprising sled and toboggan runs, an icy body slide, climbing towers, giant snowballs and an ice cave. Ski Dubai is also the house for a number of penguins who are let out of their enclosures several times a day for the visitors. This is a fun place to visit by both adults and kids.

4. Desert Safari Dubai -

Desert safari is a fun experience to have in Dubai because of its vast desert and top notch vehicles. Desert Safari in Dubai is divided into morning Desert safari and evening desert safari. The desert safari in the morning is for people who don’t have much time and have busy evening, they should try the morning safari of Dubai for adventure and fun bashing experience. The Desert safari car will pick you up in the morning at 9:00 AM and take you directly to the massive desert. There you will have a fun ride of 20 minutes with awesome dune bashing with their experienced drivers and then head back to the camp. In the camp you can also get the options to try sand skiing, quad biking and camel rides. The supply of water and soft drinks are also complimentary at the camp. After the fun experience of desert safari, the car drops you to the hotel. This fun desert experience takes just 2 hours of yours. The evening desert safari is a must for everyone who is visiting Dubai. It will be a memory to share with your family and friends when you reach back to home. The desert safari is recommended highly and it is worth every penny spent on it. The tour starts in the afternoon in the desert of Dubai with many different stops for photography. During this desert safari you experience an exciting dune drive while arriving at the first destination you will see a beautiful sunset. The group stops to watch the beautiful sunset and after that it is continued with Dune bashing to the final destination, which is the campsite. There you have the opportunity to do a fun camel ride, sand boarding and many other things to do. After the wonderful desert ride you get to enjoy a delicious barbecue dinner with the fire show in the camp.

5. Al Qudra Cycling Track -

The Al Qudra Cycling track is for cycling lovers, Al Quadra Cycle Track is situated in Dubai is a paradise for cycling lovers. The long 100km track is always filled with cycling enthusiasts from many different parts of the world due to the city limit are not legalizing cycling. But Al Qudra cycling track is open to those from Middle Aged Men in Lycra to Sunday Morning Strollers. The two start points are, the first appears when one drives the eastern out of Dubai, near Arabian Ranches and Studio city. From there one can pedal the cycle to a long and straight track that goes along the Al Quadra Road. The second start point is situated after 18km of cycling from the first point and it is a hive for activities. Here are the Trek Bicycle Store which offers rental services and mechanics and there are coffee shops and medical center too. Al Qudra Cycling Track is a very popular destination among Dubai’s adventure seekers and specially for cyclers. The track is 86Km long, with some amazing views of the desert where the riders may also catch some glimpse of the local wildlife of the area. The Al Qudra cycling track is popular among both the professionals and casual riders. The track is frequently visited by both group and solo riders. It is also visited by weekly many cycling clubs and world-class athletes visiting Dubai.

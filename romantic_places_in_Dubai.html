16 romantic places in Dubai

A vacation with your partner in Dubai can prove to be the most beautiful Experience. This city has a lot to offer for couples looking for a time to spend solely with their better halves. Here we share with you the list of 16 romantic places in Dubai to create some unforgettable moments:

1. Pierchic, Madinat Jumeirah –

A perfect place for a beautiful evening with your partner. The mesmerizing scenic beauty of this place sets a perfect location for a night date. The soothing ambiance gives you a chance to open up your hearts. Relish the delectable seafood while looking over the Arabian Gulf in the backdrop of the setting sun.

2.The Eauzone, The Royal Mirage-

The magnificent décor of this place offers a tranquil atmosphere where you can stop rushing and just relax. Enjoy the amazing European cuisine with that little Asian twist. Dining at the Eauzone is like a dream come true. The serene music in the background and the overall calm atmosphere proves to be a perfect romantic setting. Walkthrough the wooden deck to get a glimpse of the far and wide Arabian Gulf being by the side of your adorable better half.

3.Fish Beach Taverna - Le Méridien Mina Seyahi Beach Resort & Marina-

This is another chic place for dinner at the beach. The polite and eager to serve the staff of this place adds another glitter to your dining experience. Relish the fantastic Turkish, Greek, or Mediterranean cuisines against the backdrop of the sea. The hustle and bustle of the city are left at your back and you just get to enjoy the skyline of Dubai. Gazing at the night sky with a loved one in such an enthralling setting gives you an experience you’d cherish for a lifetime.

4. Fun at Kite Beach-

If you're looking forward to enjoying an entire day by the sea, Kite Beach offers this opportunity. Step in the morning into some most exciting sports by the beach like tennis and volleyball. Relax on the seashore when it’’s noon. When the sun gets ready to set, you'd get ready to go. Enjoy the thrilling water sports like Kitesurfing and wakeboarding. Let the evening culminate into a feast when you savor Dubai’s fast food.

5. Wafi City’s Theatre-

An all-time favorite of couples is watching a romantic movie. So how about heading to Wafi city’s rooftop movie theatre and spend a beautiful night watching a classic hit. Look up at the night sky and let the cool wind embrace you as you enjoy some precious moments with your loved one.

6. Overnight Dubai Safari - Luxury Dinner in Desert-

Dubai safari includes a plethora of activities to engage in with your loved one. The bonfire, belly dancing, and tanoura dancing make up for an exciting and fun-filled evening. The entire night is lit up with adventure sports like sand skiing, dune bashing, camel riding, and quad biking. It’s a place to get the first-hand experience of the authentic Arabian art in the form of henna painting and tattoo painting. After all the fun, how about relishing a luxury dinner amidst the desert. The awesome restaurants there have it all to cater to you.

7. Romantic Moments in A Hot Air Balloon

Well if you wish to soar high and touch the sky while holding your partner's hand, your dream too can be realized in Dubai. Balloon Adventure Emirates LLC (the most trusted and experienced tour operator in this field) can fulfill your desire. Get a brilliant view of the shining desert sand. Explore the desert wildlife kept untouched by the human hand. You’d be amused to have a look at the Oryx and the gazelle. It'll be a great idea to have breakfast up in the sky as you pass by the golden sand in your aerial chariot!

8. Lock bridge at the Yards-

This place in Dubai is a lovers hotspot. Couples throng here to put a lock at this bridge with their names written on it. It symbolizes that their love would stay on till eternity. So why don’t you too make it a part of your itinerary to surprise your partner?

9. A Spa Date-

Book a couple’s in one of the luxurious spas in Dubai. Get rid of all the weariness of this exciting trip by being soaked in those aromatic essential oils. Your partner would love this amazing gift for sure. There are plenty of these superb spas like Talise Spa, Heavenly Spa, The spa, and Anantara spa.

10. A Limo Ride-

Enjoy a lavish ride with your beloved in a Limousine. What would be a better way to explore the city than through a limo ride. The luxurious interiors, amazing sound system, and skilled chauffeurs offered by the traveling agencies make your ride an unforgettable experience.

11. Miracle Garden-

What can be more romantic than an entire flower garden. This place is a must-visit for all lovely couples. The wide array of flowers arranged in every imaginable form is what best describes the miracle garden. A perfect place for photoshoots, Miracle Garden provides you an ambiance to open up your hearts to your loved ones.

12. Dinner Date On-board-

It is never an old fashion to set out on a boat ride with a partner. That’s the most classic date and Dubai has it all to make this experience enthralling. You can book traditional Arabic dows in Dubai Creek. What makes for a perfect evening is the unlimited drinks and on boat entertainment. A peaceful dinner can also be planned across the Dubai marina or one can book cruises of the Bateaux Dubai.

13. A Romantic Walk-

Whether you agree or not, but the fact that simple is extraordinary can’t be denied. A stroll through the gorgeous city hand in hand with your significant other can be an experience like never before. A walk in the Downtown, which is a hub of Dubai’s biggest hotels and the Burj Khalifa can make up for a romantic walk. For a tranquil experience, wander on the Jumeirah beach admiring the magnificent Dubai skyline.

14. Explorer the love lake in Al Qudra-

This heart-shaped beauty has been voted as one of the top romantic places in Dubai. There are two lakes carved out in the shape of two interconnected hearts and trees that spell the word “love”. You'd get to witness the magnificent wildlife of this place. A visit here is free of charge so it can make up for an affordable date night. This desert Oasis is one of the best BBQ spots. You are sure to fall in love with the astonishing beauty and layout of this place. So, whether it be a proposal, your first anniversary, a honeymoon, or a wedding shoot, Dubai has it all for a perfect romantic rendezvous.

15.Pai Thai in Madinat Jumeirah -

The restaurant has an open setting and gives a spectacular view of the Burj al Arab. Visit in the evening to enjoy the fantastic lights and the music at the backdrop. The entire ambiance offers a perfect setting to propose your loved one. Sit back and gaze at the starry sky. It would be a great idea to plan an Abra ride to make this evening even more special.

16.Villa Beach-

How about dining at the beach in a natural setting. The sea breeze now and then greets you as you relax with your feet soaked in the cool sand. The magnificent view of the Burj al Arab makes for a perfect backdrop as you engage in some lovely talks with your partner. The hospitable staff of the restaurant and overall pleasant ambiance can make you forget all worries and spend some special time on the beach.

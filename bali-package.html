Day 2

FULL DAY KINTAMANI AND UBUD VILLAGE TOUR


After Breakfast at Hotel,proceed for full day Kintamani and Ubud Village tour which is one day trip to explore Kintamani and Ubud area. Enjoy watching Barong and Keris dance first and then visit Celuk for gold silver art, Batuan for fine art, Tirta Empul (holy spring temple), Kintamani with a beautiful view of Mount Batur Volcano, Tegalalang rice terrace and last is Ubud monkey forest.


Pick up from your hotel at around 0900 hrs and visit Kintamani where you will be fascinated by the lush panoramic views of Mount Batur—an active volcanic site and its surroundings. Continue to visit the sacred Ubud Monkey Forest. The Ubud Monkey Forest describes its mission as conservation of the area.


The next stop is Puri Saren Ubud or more popular name as the Bali Ubud Royal Palace is an Ubud Kingdom Palace with beautiful Balinese traditional houses as a residence of Ubud King. Later visit Ubud Art market, locally referred to as 'Pasar Seni Ubud' located opposite the Puri Saren Royal Ubud Palace and is open daily. Here you can find beautiful silk scarves, lightweight shirts, handmade woven bags, baskets or hats, statues, kites and many other hand-crafted goods.


Later stop at Tirta Empul Temple where for more than thousand years, Balinese worshipers have been drawn and whose sacred spring is said to have been created by Indra and to have curative properties. The Tour will end by Shopping Drop at DFS Mall Bali Galleria famous for cheapest world branded stuff in town.


Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 3

FULL DAY UNIVERSAL STUDIO TOUR


After Breakfast at Hotel,Tanah Lot Temple is one of Indonesia’s most popular religious attractions. Commonly referred to as the “temple of the rock,” this temple off the coast of Bali is set upon a black-stone peninsula that juts into rippling waters. Incredible ocean views, clear mountain air, and a deep spiritual connection draw visitor to this unique sight. Tanah Lot is one of nine directional temples on the coast of Bali island that were built to protect the island from evil spirits. During the temple commemoration, the farmers will have holly water that they sprinkle to their rice field.


Overnight Stay at Hotel


Pura Tanah Lot temple is a extremely popular tourist destination. It does have cultural significance to the Balinese, situated on theTanah Lot which is the small island floating on the sea. This temple is very famous among tourist destinations in Bali with spectacular view of sunset and cultural icon for photography. It is one of the most important and venerated sea temples for the Balinese. Nowadays, there are some eateries that will open up for sunset drinks and meals.There are also many souvenir stalls surrounding the area, catering to the tourists.

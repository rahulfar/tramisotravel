12 best bakeries in Dubai for your sweet craving

Dubai has a lot of options for your sweet delight. Try out these 12 best bakeries in Dubai for your sweet craving :

1. Papparoti:-

You can find their outlets at various places like the Dubai Mall and the Sunset Mall. The mouth-watering dishes are a must-try for all foodies. They are renowned for their exquisite buns especially their hazelnut buns, crunchy outside and soft inside. Amazing place to enjoy cakes and coffees. Also, try their refreshing ginger lemon tea. You'll fall in love with their excellent customer service and a beautiful ambiance.

2.Mangolia Bakery:

A perfect place for all those having a sweet tooth. All of their preparations are simply amazing, whether it be the cupcakes or the puddings, you'll like it all. There’s a great variety of flavors to choose from. The staff is really helpful and friendly. All their items are freshly prepared on the premises only. You have both eat-in and take away options. Try out their banana pudding, cheesecakes, choco, and vanilla cupcakes and also the devil’s cake cupcake.

3.Laduree-

A peaceful place to enjoy a nice coffee and munch on some yummy snacks. This café and patisserie at Fashion Avenue, Dubai Mall is known for the courteous service of the staff. Everything about Laduree is just awesome whether it be the ambiance, the variety, or their presentation. Sit, enjoy, and relax after a shopping spree at the Mall. Try their chocolates, croissants, coffee, latte, French toast, and macron. Their taste and style are simply exquisite.

4. Ben's Cookies-

Enjoy a great variety of yummy chocolate cookies. Double chocolate, dark chocolate, white chocolate with macadamia nuts, milk chocolate with peanut butter, every preparation is delightful at Ben's. All of their cookies are freshly baked and there’s a small cozy seating arrangement too. Try out their soft drinks, chick chips, and coffee also. You’ll get hot cookies for 12 dirhams each, but you can avail a discount on bulk purchase.

5. Bouchon Bakery-

This restaurant by Michelin star chef has an impressive menu. The overall ambiance makes you feel as you're in Paris. The staff is very polite and friendly. Their best preparations are cinnamon caramel bun, eggs royal, almond croissant, and pain au raisin. You’ll be served good and gourmet dessert and the complimentary Macaron that you get with the coffee is again wonderful.

6. Hummingbird Bakery-

It is the best place to buy celebration cakes. The quality and design of their cakes are enthralling. The service of the staff is just excellent. They greet you always with the smile and make your visit a memorable one. The drinks too are just magical. Their cupcakes are really special and everyone loves them whether it be the kids or the grown-ups!

7. Cinnabon-

The lovely Cinnabon go well with a coffee at this Café. It's a great sight to see the pastry chef roll out fresh buns in front of you. Their efforts and focus to maintain hygiene, quality, and output is quite evident. This is a must-visit place with your tour mates for a fresh and warm treat.

8. Gourmandises-

Situated in the Le Meridian Hotel, it is a nice cozy place to enjoy with friends. Their coffee and croissants are just fabulous. It is not too busy and you can also spend some peaceful time with yourself, a reading session probably. Also, this is a nice place for business meetings. You’ll get great service from the polite and respectful staff.

9. Paul's Patisserie-

Try out this French-themed café in Abu Dhabi for a joyful evening. Their services are excellent. You can even enjoy your snacks in the outdoor and could visit in the morning too as the early hours are not that busy. They use French ingredients in their preparations which is why their items have a unique taste. The French furniture and eclectic furnishing add charm to the place. You're attended by the friendly and professional staff at Paul’s. You can enjoy chicken and avocado sandwiches and some great desserts. Even the salads here are dressed in French style and you sure to enjoy anything that you order.

10. Krispy Kreme Doughnuts-

Kids always love a visit to this place. Freshly made doughnuts cheer up anybody, isn’t it? They have an extensive menu and a great variety of flavors for you to choose from. It is one of the best places to consider for a nice cup of coffee with doughnuts at affordable prices. Glazed doughnuts and doughnuts with hazelnut fillings are a must-try. All of their items are freshly baked and there’s a smiling and attentive staff to help you out with anything you want.

11. KVivel Patisserie-

Their signature dish is the Turkish delight scented with rose water. Each square has dried edible flowers or herbs that gives a special taste to the dish. The sugar-coated almonds are a treat to the soul, which you can also get packaged in gift boxes for your loved ones back home. This place is renowned for its unique preparations. Chocolates and candies are also amazing. But one should not miss out on the Persian and Middle Eastern styled teas with dates and figs in it.

12. Project Cupcake-

This bakery keeps to its name and serves one of the best cupcakes in Abu Dhabi. Carrot, red velvet, Nutella, queen B cupcake, all of them are magical. Also, the unicorn flavored cupcakes with their fancy colors are a must-try. The cupcakes are indeed a nice gifting item and you can have them packed in decorative boxes. The staff is really helpful and would suggest some great delicacies to try. Project Cupcake is also a nice place to relish some cold drinks or milkshakes.

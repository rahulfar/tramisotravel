20 Best things to buy from Dubai

Dubai is a shopper's paradise where you can purchase from anything to everything. The best deals, amazing souvenirs, and ethnic Arabian flavor, the Bazaars of Dubai have a lot in offing.

1. Aladdin Lamps::-

Well the Arabian countries have a lot of folktales to narrate to its natives as well as it’s visitors and one of the most famous of these is the story of Aladdin and his Chirag ( lamp). This story is indeed an all-time favorite. When you visit Abu Dhabi, it'll be a great idea to shop a few Aladdin lamps to gift to your loved ones back home. These brass lamps with detailed carving are great showpiece items.
Where To Buy- Karama Souq, Street No. 18b, Dubai UAE; Al- Karama Market, Dubai – United Arab Emirates

2.Ayyam Gallery-Camel Milk Chocolate:

It is one of the most delicious things that you can take back home from Abu Dhabi. Earlier it was present only in Dubai but now it is also being exported to Europe and Asian countries. The Nassma camel milk chocolate is available in many varieties: whole milk, macadamia, and spiced ( Arabia ) chocolates. Youngsters or adults, everyone loves chocolates and they are never outdated. So, won’t it be a great idea to let your friends have camel milk chocolates from Dubai this time!
Where To Buy- Al Nassma kiosks at Dubai Mall, Burj Al Arab, Burj Khalifa, and Duty-free shops

3.Lanterns-

If you wish to give a classy souvenir to your dear ones after returning from Dubai, you should surely go shopping for some lanterns. You can buy a few to adorn your home too. The craft and labor that goes into their making are commendable and there’s no match to the authentic look that a lantern carries with it. Choose some beautifully designed lanterns like those with small bits of glass woven into them as they shine even when they’re not lit.
Where To Buy- Dragon Mart, Al Awir Road, Dubai 7567, United Arab Emirates; Karama Souq, Street No. 18b, Dubai UAE

4. Dubai Dates-

Dates are indeed an ideal souvenir to take home from this Arabian land since dates are a prominent part of Middle Eastern culture. They are rich source of iron, magnesium, calcium, and many other essential elements. So, dates are perfect for a tasty and healthy treat. Moreover, they're not very expensive. Also, in Dubai, you can find around 20 different varieties of dates like Sokari, Kholas, Madjool, etc. all of which are palatable.
Where To Buy- Dates souk at Deira Food Market for fresh dates; Supermarkets for packed dates

5. Arabic Attar-

These are perfumed oils prepared using distilled flower petals. They have a strong, exotic fragrance that is very pleasant. Certain spices and oils are added for a magnificent aroma and for a great finishing to the overall fragrance, a bit of sandalwood oil are used. You must choose the one that suits your skin color and personality. If you are not sure about this matter, better go for branded options in the famous Hind Al Oud and the Amouage.
Where To Buy- Perfume Souk at Deira, Perfume shops in malls and the airport

6. Persian rugs and carpets-

These amazing floor covers are indeed one of the best things that you can buy from Dubai. The Persian rugs and carpets are simply beautiful, classy, and one of the best home décor items. Their intricate designs and fine detailing is a reflection of great artwork. The best thing is these souvenirs are really budget-friendly.
Where To Buy- National Iranian Carpets at Souq Madinat Jumeirah; Persian Carpet House at Souq Madinat Jumeirah, Dubai Mall, Mirdif City Centre and Souq al Bahar

7. Lucky stones -

These customizable stones are really splendid and indeed make for a great gift. They are selected on the basis of the month of your birth and are usually embedded in gold or platinum rings or even pendulums. There are a plethora of majestic designs and especially the ones with Arabian artwork are really enthralling. These stones are believed to bring good luck in one's life. So, shop for these on your visit to Abu Dhabi and return home as a more fortunate person!
Where To Buy- Pearlicious in Covent Garden, Dubai Marina; Gold & Diamond Park, Office No. 213, Building No 4, 4th Interchange, Sheik Zayed Road, United Arab Emirates

8. Oudh and Bakhoor-

Oudh and Bakhoor are an indispensable part of Middle Eastern culture. Shop for these mesmerizing fragrances as they make up for a great gift. The traditional fragrances are indeed distinct and special. Oudh is derived from Agarwood resin and is a bit expensive, but the magical aroma that it creates is worth the money. On the other hand, Bakhoor refers to Agarwood chips which when burned spread out a very pleasing aroma. So, try out some soothing gift for your loved ones!
Where To Buy- Perfume Souk at Deira for Oudh scents and oils; Perfume shops in malls; Spice Souk for Bakhoor

9. Hookah-

Hookahs are indeed a special and extravagant gift that you can take from Abu Dhabi. They are quite prominent and common in Arab households. Even when simply kept in a corner, they give your home a classy and traditional look. And undoubtedly, they are the best things to chill and relax. You can find fancy, expensive Hookahs in some shops in Dubai but the cheap ones are also available.
Where To Buy- Smokers’ Centre outlets; Carrefour Hypermarket; Dubai souks

10. Pashmina shawls-

There cannot be anything better than a traditional Pashmina shawl for a gift. It is indeed a memorable gift to give to your loved ones. These are some of the most famous souvenirs indeed. The classy, stylish Pashminas are available in a lot of vibrant colors. The real ones are really expensive but if you’re looking for a budget- friendly item, you can shop for silk or viscose versions of the same.
Where To Buy- Smokers’ Centre outlets; Carrefour Hypermarket; Dubai souks

11. Kava Pots and coffee sets-

Kava is absolutely pure coffee and has a very special place in Arabian culture. This black and strong coffee are served in fancy pots and designer cups. The cutlery is usually lined with gold or silver and the precious ones are even embedded with semi-precious stones. This cutlery item is one of the best things you can buy in Abu Dhabi. Anyone whom you gift this would surely love it.
Where To Buy- Dubai Mall, Financial Centre Road, Downtown Dubai, Near Burj Khalifa, United Arab Emirates; Mall of the Emirates, Al Barsha 1 – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

12. Gold and Diamond jewelry-

Gold is a must-buy item in this “ city of gold” since you get it at very reasonable rates as compared to other parts of the world. One ought to shop for the special, intricately designed jewelry items in Abu Dhabi for oneself. And precious jewelry is always a great item to gift to your beloved.
Where To Buy- Dubai Gold Souk, Gold and Diamond Park, Joyalukkas outlets, jewelry stores

13. Arabian Coffee-

Arabian coffee is known for its strong flavor and all coffee lovers must Shop for its Dubai markets. Your experience of Dubai Street food is incomplete until you’ve tasted the Arabian coffee and it’s indeed a great idea to take home this rare and loving coffee item.
Where To Buy- Dubai Coffee Museum, Dubai Souks, and major supermarkets

14. Electronic items-

Dubai has one of the best electronic markets in the world. You can get some amazing deals as your favorite electronic items are usually available at a discounted price. So, before leaving for home buy your favorite electronic items at the exclusive markets. You'll receive the best quality home appliances like refrigerator, washing machine, or even your television set. If you visit during the festive season, you’ll be lured by the great offers!
Where To Buy- Al-Fahidi Street in Bur Dubai area, Sharaf DG store or other electronics stores

15. Dubai spices-

Flavorsome Dubai spices are renowned all over the world. These are sold at the local market at very affordable prices. You'll get the best quality turmeric, red chili, cardamom, or any spice you name you'll find it here. Take the flavor of Dubai to your kitchen and spice up your curries and other recipes.
Where To Buy- Spice Souk, Supermarkets

16. Incense holders-

Incense holders are part of Dubai's culture and hospitality. The soothing aroma brightens up the entire atmosphere. When you burn the special Arabic incense sticks in it, your house would have the heavenly fragrance and your guests would find it difficult to leave your place! Their intricate and traditional Arabic design makes for a great showpiece and you can gift it your loved ones too. These days, the electric incense burners are quite prevalent.
Where To Buy: Spice Souq, Sikkat Al Khail Road, Al Ras, Dubai.

17. Traditional Arab shoes-

You can try out the traditional Arab shoes that have a great variety for you to choose from. They are usually available in slim fit and taper at the toes. Their intricate design is really special whether it be the thread work, beads, baubles, or cloth bills, all of them are majestic. You can choose according to your outfit and preferences. So put on these special Arab shoes to get the Aladdin to feel!
Where To Buy: The Cloth Souk, Bur Dubai, Dubai

18. Khanjar-

Khanjar is one of the most important elements in the attire of Emirati men. These are generally worn over men’s dishdashas and are curved in shape with silver and gold embossed on it. They were originally made out of rhino horns, but due to the amendment in- laws, they are now mostly made of wood. Their handles are usually made of silver, marble, or sandalwood. Generally, the sheath of the dagger is decorated with silver-threaded weave. Overall it's a great souvenir from the Arabian land and is simply classy that would adorn your home.
Where To Buy: Yemen Pavilion, Global Village

19. Jewel Chest-

To complement your purchase of jewelry, a jewel chest is a must-have too. Generally made from sandalwood, these chests come in different colors, designs, and sizes. These are spacious and provide the perfect solution for organizing your jewelry. The Arabic styled jewel chests are intricately adorned with precious or semi-precious stones, brass, and silver. These Jewel boxes also make a great gifting option or simply keep them with you as a souvenir.
Where To Buy – Dubai Gold Souq, Dubai, UAE

20. Quran stands-

You can shop for some religious items in this Muslim city and Quran stands are great items to consider. They are usually made of wood with marble inlays. Many stands have verses from the holy book inscribed on them. They are available in many forms. One is the collapsible stands that can easily be adjusted and there are the simpler ones that are used to read while in a sitting position. Their magnificent design is what makes it a great souvenir from this great city.
Where To Buy – Al Abbes Islamic Books Shop, Sarah or Makkah Islamic Book Center

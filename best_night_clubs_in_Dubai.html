10 best night clubs in Dubai

What makes Dubai the most sought-after tourist destination is that it lacks nothing. When it comes to nightlife, Dubai is replete with one of the best night clubs to offer you that party vibe. Check out on these 10 best nightclubs in Dubai for a memorable night out experience:

1. Armani/Privé Nightclub –

One of the finest clubs in Dubai, Armani/ Prive nightclub is located in the Burj Khalifa, which makes it a must-visit. Hospitality at its best is what best describes this place. This fancy and luxurious club will drive you mad with the amazing DJ and you can’t stop yourself from dancing the night out. The quality of drinks and food keep to the brand name.
Address: Sheik Mohammed Bin Rashid Blvd No 1, Burj Khalifa, Armani Hotel Dubai, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Timings: 11.00 PM to 03.00 AM; Closed on Mondays and Wednesdays.

2. Cavalli Club -

It is the oldest running club in Dubai. The attractive, charming ambiance is a true reflection of the Roberto Cavalli style. The spectacular interior décor complete with low hanging chandeliers, Swarovski crystals, and the enchanting furnishing is what drives great celebrities to this place. The international cuisine and the mind-blowing DJ are the highlights of the Cavalli Club. The award-winning bar team is ready to serve you. The mesmerizing live shows by top international artists like Kid Ink, Sean Paul, and Bob Sinclair make up for a memorable night.
Address: Sheikh Zayed Road, Fairmont Hotel
Timings: 8:30 PM to 3:00 AM; Open Every Day

3. Base -

This place has all luxuries of a premium lounge and is one of the top class nightclubs in Dubai. Known for inviting international artists for performances, a visit here would give you the concert vibe. There is ladies’ night twice a week. The pyrotechnic theatre feature within the bar is a treat to the eye. Visit here to relish the mocktails and cocktails while enjoying the kaleidoscopic ambiance.
Address: D3, Ras Al Khor Road
Timings: 10:00 PM - 4:00 PM; Open from Thursday – Saturday only.

4. Club 360 -

This award-winning night Club offers a perfect night out experience. The spectacular view of the Burj al Arab and the Persian Gulf offers a panoramic view. You get to enjoy the soft lounge music while relishing cocktails amidst the scenic beauty of the sea and the magnificent skyscrapers. A perfect place for partying indeed!
Address:  Jumeira Road, Jumeira Beach Hotel
Timings: 5:00 PM to 03:00 AM; Open Everyday

5. Club Boudoir -

It is one of the finest places for a perfect night out in Abu Dhabi. But mind you, this place is strict with its dress codes. You’re expected to conduct yourself in an organized manner. The best part about club Boudoir is that it offers it’s coolest drinks at the most reasonable rates. Thursday night is mostly a Bollywood night. The amazing services of the courteous staff are really unforgettable.
Address:  Al Reem 1, 3 St
Timings: 10:00 PM - 3:00 AM; Open Everyday

6. Billionaire Mansion Nightclub -

Experience royalty at it’s best in this most luxurious nightclub of Dubai. The Billionaire Mansion has it all to entertain its guests. Whether it be the outdoor dance terrace or the extravagant dinners. The karaoke night here is a true delight. This place is renowned for having international DJ and artist performances. The highlight is the custom made champagne show.
Address: Taj Dubai, Business Bay
Timings: 7:00 PM - 3:00 AM; Open from Tuesday to Sunday

7. White Dubai Nightclub -

It is a rooftop nightclub and is truly spectacular with ultra-modern design to adorn the already vibrant ambiance. It has been rated as the world’s 15 th club on the list of DT MAG top 100 clubs. It is indeed a busy Club but the well trained and polite staff make your experience a remarkable one. On DJs, they have different themes at each night from hip hop/R&B to all types of electrifying music.
Address: Al Meydan Road, Meydan Racecourse Grandstand, Rooftop Venue
Timings: 11:00 PM to 3:00 PM Open from Thursdays to Saturday

8. Chi @ The Lodge Nightclub -

It is a multi-themed venue and is one of the most popular nightclubs in Dubai. This is a spacious club and has got a large setting to tap your feet on the variety of music being played,
Address: Near the American Hospital, Oud Metha
Timings: 10:00 PM to 3:00 PM; Open Every day.

9. People by Crystal nightclub -

It is one of the topmost nightclubs in Dubai, where you'd find a wonderful seating arrangement. It is renowned for its ladies’ nights. The DJ is really superb who’s smart enough to sense the mood of the people. The stringent security around may seem a little troublesome to a few, but the overall ambiance is magnificent.
Address: Umm Hurair 2, 18th floor Umm Hurair Road, (2.6 Kilometres away from the Dubai Creek)
Timings: 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM; It is open on Tuesday and Thursday to Sunday

10. Nasimi - Atlantis The Palm -

Well here's an opportunity for you to party at the soft sands. It would be a great clubbing experience on a 7 Km long beach, home to Atlantis the Palm. Watch the sunset from a rented cabana for a perfect end of the evening culminating into a beautiful night of dance, music, enthralling performances, delectable seafood and the great variety of cocktails.
Timings: 11 AM to 1:30 PM

10 best Art Galleries to see in Dubai

Dubai is home to one of the finest art galleries in the world. If you admire magnificent artworks, check out the following art galleries in Abu Dhabi

1. The Third Line:-

The Third Line art gallery is one of the top-notch contemporary art hubs that showcases the best of works of Middle Eastern artists. It is a platform for experienced as well as emerging art pioneers to represent their artifacts. The third line also hosts nonprofit programs to generate interest in the region. “Works on Paper” is a book wing that exclusively publishes artists' books. Books published to date are Presence by photographer Lamya Gargash, In Absentia by Tarek – Al – Ghoussein, and many more.

2.Ayyam Gallery-

It is host to a wide variety of fantastic artwork and is admired by tourists’ from all across the globe. The art shows are a happening thing at Ayyam Gallery and every time you visit this place, you’ll get to witness the works of a new artist that they've launched. The staff is approachable and is ready to discuss the artworks at the display. Their mandate is to spread art far and wide. Their multilingual publishing division and the custodianship program offers great opportunities to emerging artists.

3.Lawrie Shabibi-

It is one of the most well rounded contemporary art galleries in Dubai that is a highlight in the annual Art Dubai event. This art gallery has hosted some great works by well- renowned artists like Nadia Kaabi- Linke, Adel Abidin, Mounir Fatmi, and Nick Devereaux and it is one of the miatbsought after places to purchase classic artworks. At Lawrie Shabibi, you’ll find works by artists hailing from the Middle East and North Africa in particular as this art hub works towards the long term development of these artists.

4. Opera Gallery-

It is one of the most elegant art galleries in Dubai that hosts artists from all genres, whether it be graffiti artists or those who've designed pop music album covers. If you admire the works of George Morton Clark, Blek Le Rat, and Keith Haring, this place is a must- visit for you. At night, the Opera Gallery is open for hosting private events in a magnificent and prestigious setting. An internationally established gallery at DIFC has even played host to big names like Pablo Picasso and Bernard Buffet.

5. Green Art Gallery-

This art gallery focuses on contemporary art from across the Middle East, North Africa, and South Asia. Notable artists include Turkey's Hale Tenger, Iran's Kamrooz Aram, Palestine born Shadi Habib Allah, Pakistan's Seher Shah, and Venezuelan Alessandro Balteo – Yazbeck. The display at the gallery is really spectacular and a visit here is indeed an enriching experience for all art lovers. Green Art Gallery regularly collaborates with curators and guest artists to present their works for the first time in the region. It has participated in various international and regional art fairs, including Art Dubai, Moving Image Fair in London, and Artissima Art Fair.

6. XVA-

It is one of the most famous art Galleries in Dubai that showcases the majestic culture of various Arab countries. It even holds exhibitions all across the globe. You'll find splendid art pieces in the entire chain of XVA's hotels, café, boutique apart from the fantastic gallery. Set within the heart of Al Fahidi Quarter, the entire XVA complex is a sight to behold.

7. Gallery 1-

Here you can find a plethora of wonderful souvenirs to take back home for your loved ones. There’s a wide range of flipbooks, posters, notecards, etc. and they mostly feature the enchanting Arabian culture. The superior quality of all it's products is what makes it the most sought after art hub. The art and passion of some of the well-renowned artists adorn each corner of Gallery 1 and it thus becomes a must-visit place for tourists.

8. Cuadro Fine Art Gallery-

It showcases the talent of some exceptional contemporary artists. The emphasis is mainly on four major aspects: exhibitions, consultation, residency, and education. The Cuadro Education Programme has collaborated with the exhibition team and it organizes panel discussions, lectures, and workshops. Also, the gallery strongly aims at showing their artwork in international art communities.

9. The Empty Quarter-

The Empty Quarter is established by HRH Reem Al-Faisal and Safa Al- Hamed. This art gallery is exclusively dedicated to fine art photography and promotes the work of international photographers including Al-Moutasim Al Maskery, Steve McCurry, Marc Riboud, and Bruno Barbey. It is a paradise for all of those who are interested in photography. There’s a lot to learn, explore, and admire at this place and you can spend hours engrossed in some superb snapshots.

10. Andakulova Art Gallery-

Founded by Natalya Andakulova, this art gallery seeks to promote Central Asian culture in the Middle East. It features splendid contemporary visual arts and there's remarkable work by artists especially from Uzbekistan. The Andakulova Art Gallery also builds relations between the artists, writers, specialists, and collectors to achieve the objective of dialogue between Central Asia and the Middle East. The artifacts depict some most memorable events from the world and art history. The art night events are organized throughout the year and it's a great place to socialize with art enthusiasts. You can also buy an art piece to adorn your home or office.

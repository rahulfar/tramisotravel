Twelve top Shisha lounge bars in Dubai

Shisha is an incredible aspect of the culture of Abu Dhabi. If you too are a Shisha fan and look forward to an unforgettable shisha evening in Dubai, you're at the right place. Here, we share with you 12 top shisha lounge bars in Dubai

1. Smoky Beach –

This place is just by the beach and is one of the best places in the city to enjoy Shisha. The overall ambiance is quite relaxed and calming. Added to this, smoky Beach has great options for drinks and foods. Friendly staff and prompt service would make your visit a joyful experience.
Address: The Beach, Jumeirah Beach Residence, Dubai
Opening times: Daily, 9 am – 5 am

2.Shades-

A resplendent lounge and bar in Dubai, Shades is a place where you can enjoy the splendid view of the infinity pool while enjoying Shisha. The chill- out lounge provides a great dining experience. This place is renowned for great beverages like smoothies, iced teas, and juices. In this spectacular outdoor lounge, you can enjoy shish until 1 am but anyone below 21 is not allowed after 10 pm.
Address: Address Dubi Marina, Al Marsa Street, 66, Dubai, 32923, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Daily, 9 am – 1 pm, shisha served from 6 pm to 1 am

3.Al Sarab Rooftop lounge-

The rooftop ambiance makes it the perfect place to relish shisha. The splendid view of the city and the overall relaxed ambiance attracts people The rooftop lounge. The décor is really classy and to make the shisha evening an unforgettable one there's traditional music playing under the low lighting. You'd be delighted by the service and hospitality of the staff. This could be a great place to celebrate some special occasions.
Address: Al Qudra Road, Opposite Endurance City – Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Daily from 4:00 pm – 1:00 am (last orders at 12.45 am)

4. Mariya restaurant and cafe-

What makes it a sought after the place is the fact that it is open 24 hours. The best way to enjoy the cheerful atmosphere is with a group of friends. The view outside is simply awesome and you cannot stop loving the delectable dishes of this place. To add to the delight of its guests Marbaiya restaurant and café has karaoke evenings on Mondays and Fridays.
Address: Al Daai street, Marina Walk, next to Grosvenor House Dubai, Dubai, United Arab
Emirates Opening times: 24 hour

5. The Kana café-

This spacious café can accommodate around 350 people and is popular among Shisha fans. Apart from the ordinary shisha flavors that you usually get at other places, what makes Kana café different is the unique flavors like Red bull and cappuccino that you can enjoy in Shisha. People also generally love the juices and tea here. The enchanting décor and the exciting board games make up for a special evening.
Address: Fifty-One Tower, Business Bay, Dubai
Opening times: Saturday through Wednesday 10 am – 2 am; Thursday and Friday 10 am – 3 am

6. Piccolo Mondo Bay-

Visit here to spend a stylish evening as you relish your Shisha. This is a place where you can enjoy an outside view under perfect lighting sitting on cozy sofas or make yourself comfortable in an eclectic interior setting. The amazing menu list of the Asian – Italian cuisines can add to a perfect evening. You'll be pleased with the service of the hospitable staff here.
Address: Dubai Marina, Marina Tower, Basement Level, Dubai Marina
Opening times: 24 hours

7. SIKKA café

This is one place where you have a variety of shisha flavors to choose from. Moreover, the Middle Eastern cuisine ( shakshuka, Manakish, etc.) at SIKKA café is a must-try. The delectable mocktails are highlights of this place. A vibrant and welcoming ambiance is created by the décor team who've put up some great color combinations in lighting, floor tiles, and even the color of the chairs. The prompt service of the staff is really commendable.
Address: La Mer South, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Daily, 8 am – 12 am (1 am on Thursday and Friday)

8. Buhayra lounge

It is an outdoor Shisha bar located by the swimming pool. To get the luxurious Arabian dining experience, this place is the best fit. Not only do you get a solid Arabic menu with all those traditional Tabbouleh, hummus, and muzzes, but an Arabic décor as well. The place is adorned with styled tents, Palm trees, and fairy lights to take you to a dream Land as you enjoy your Shisha. For an enriching experience, there’s live music that is truly heart touching.
Address: Ground Level, Palace Downtown, Dubai
Opening times: Daily, 8 am – 2 am, last orders at 1 am

9. Shimmers-

This is a Shisha bar at the beach that can offer you that perfect evening that you dream to have in Dubai. The sunset view is breathtaking with the Burj Al Arab at the backdrop. There's great variety in Shisha that you can enjoy here. It'll be a great idea to be here with friends and celebrate some special occasion.
Address: Madinat Jumeirah, Jumeirah Beach Road, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Lunch from 12pm – 5 pm, dinner at 6.30 pm-midnight

10. Treehouse Dubai-

Also known as Greenhouse Dubai, this place is a must-visit for all Shisha lovers. This rooftop shisha lounge offers a spectacular view of the Burj Khalifa. The classy and cozy décor is greatly welcoming. A large stone fireplace that is surrounded by candles provides the perfect stylish ambiance. Here you'd also get to relish the choicest Mediterranean dishes.
Address: Taj Hotel Dubai, Burj Khalifa Street, Dubai, 8489, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Weekdays 6 pm – 2 am, weekends 6 pm – 3 am

11. Reem al Bawadi-

There are a lot of these restaurants in Dubai but the one on Jumeirah beach road is the best. It is indeed a perfect spot to enjoy classic shisha. Grab a sumptuous dinner in a pleasant atmosphere. The courteous staff could make up for a cheerful evening.
Address: Jumeirah Beach Road, Jumeirah 3, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Daily, 8 am – 2.30 am

12. The Courtyards-

This regal Shisha bar offers a Majlis style seating. Get immersed in the traditional Arabic color as you enjoy your flavors of shisha. Entering “The Courtyards” requires you to dress decently. You cannot wear short- sleeve T-shirts, shorts or flip flops here. This place is a must-visit for you to enjoy the great Arabic elegance.
Address: The Palace and Arabian Court, One&Only Royal Mirage, Dubai Marina, United Arab Emirates
Opening times: Daily, 7 pm – 1 am

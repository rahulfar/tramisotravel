10 reasons to shop in Gold Souk Dubai

Gold Souk is a traditional market in Dubai that specializes in the trade of jewelry items. People from all across the world throng to this market to have a glimpse of the magnificent jewelry items in display and why are they so fond of gold shopping at the Gold Souk, we'll reveal that to you:

1. Pure Gold-

At the Gold Souk in Dubai, the Government as well as the police are involved to ensure that only pure gold is sold. This is why people trust this gold market in Dubai. It is mandatory for Companies to ensure that their items carry a purity hallmark along with a certificate that buyers get while purchasing any gold item. So there’s no chance of fraud ever at the Gold Souk.

2.Variety and Choice-

There are over 300 jewelry shops in Dubai and a lot of different brands available for you to choose from. Each brand also has a distinctive style for design and you’ll never be disappointed when it comes to the purchase of gold. Earrings, anklets, necklaces, armlets, headgear, rings, this gold market offers you from anything to everything.

3.Safe Environment-

There's generally fear associated with the purchase of gold jewelry. But one need not worry at all while shopping for gold in Dubai. Every piece of gold is vetted by the government and local police. So, there’s absolutely no chance to ever buy stolen or fake hold from the Gold Souk. You can fearlessly go shopping for your jewelry on Dubai streets as the Dubai police are renowned for their security system and are well respected in their region. So, at the Gold Souk, you have a safe shopping experience.

4. Bargaining Freedom-

The best part about shopping for gold in Dubai is that the making charges can be bargained extensively if you’re good at the skill. Apart from shopping malls and international stores, the Deira Gold Souk allows you to bargain the making charges of gold in shops! As an example, in one of the stores a massive gold piece on display priced Dh 950,000 was brought down to 750,000. So effectively the making charges in Dubai can be bargained.

5. Cheaper Gold-

Dubai is known for being a city with exemption from taxes, and this is what makes gold a cheap commodity to purchase from here. Even after VAT was added in 2018, purchasing gold has still been cheaper compared to the local currency around the world. Also, tourists are free to claim VAT refunds in the UAE for any purchases made.

6. A well-regulated gold market-

People trust the gold from Dubai as the gold market is well regulated in the city. Regular inspections are conducted by the Dubai municipality which also regulates all merchandise sold by jewelers.

7. Transparent buying process-

The whole process of buying gold in Dubai is transparent and the customers are assured that they can never be cheated when it comes to the purchase of gold. It is mandatory for all jewelry stores to have a gold purity hallmark which certifies specific details such as karats and weight of actual gold and stones in the jewelry and the cost of the labor. This has helped to ensure that each purchase is authenticated and it can be valued anywhere in the world.

8. No import duty-

There is no import duty on gold for raw materials like TT (ten tola) and gold bars. There is a 5 percent import duty, but only on jewelry. Plus there is an additional 5 percent VAT applicable on all gold jewelry purchases. But this can be fully reclaimed at UAE airports. As a result of such a low import duty, prices of gold in Dubai are relatively less expensive and this is what attracts people to Dubai for gold shopping.

9. Jewelry imported from all over the world-

Dubai imports jewelry from all over the world, owing to the zero import duty tax on gold and TT bars and low tax on jewelry pieces. India, Singapore, Italy, Bahrain, Malaysia, and off-late Turkey are key places where Dubai imports gold and jewelry. The Dubai Municipality ensures that the purity of gold imported into Dubai is high and of the best quality.

10. Dubai gold prices follow international market rates -

There is a uniformity in pricing across jewelry stores in the emirate since the gold prices in Dubai follow international rates. This is another important reason why people from across the world wish to purchase jewelry in Dubai. Gold prices fluctuates just like the stock market. This makes it possible for buyers to take advantage of falling prices. So check gold prices every day, to strike the best possible deal.

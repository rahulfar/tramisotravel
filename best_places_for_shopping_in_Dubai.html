20 best places for shopping in Dubai

A vacation in Dubai is incomplete until one has explored a wide range of shopping places. From street retail shops to the colossal malls, Dubai has it all for shopaholics. Here we share with you the list of 20 best shopping places in Dubai:

1. The Dubai Mall –

It is the second-largest Mall in the world spread across an area of 5,00,000 sq. meters. There are more than 1200 stores and this Mall is a must-visit for all Fashion enthusiasts. It houses all international brands and is renowned for its haute couture. Shop here for high fashion and designer clothes like Versace and Burberry. There are nice options for gold jewelry too. Apart from this, there are numerous activities for all age groups. You can go to ice skating, watch cinema or munch at Rainforest café and a variety of other restaurants and café in place.
Location: Financial Center Rd - Dubai

2.Wafi Mall -

This themed Mall reflects the distinct Arabian culture and is styled after ancient Egypt. Karnak, small pyramids, and images of pharaohs adorn its interior. There are over 200 stores and the Mall houses 100s of international and regional brands. This place is a convergence of the ancient and the modern feature of Dubai. The Mall is known for its top-class spa parlors. Besides, the traditional Arab market at Souk Khan Murjan is a sight to behold. The major highlights are Matalan, the Yamaha music shop, and the Bosporus Turkish restaurant. You can shop here for designer clothes, home décor items, and kid's clothes.
Location: Oud Metha Road - Dubai

3.Deira Gold Souk-

This market finds its place in the Guinness book of world records for having the world's heaviest gold ring on display weighted 59 Kg 21 carat with 5 kg of precious stones fitted on it. You can find every kind of ornament here from traditional gold and silver to beautiful diamonds and precious stones. The wide range and variety of jewelry would mesmerize you. Even if you don’t plan to buy gold, this place is still a must-visit since even window shopping here could be an overwhelming experience. There are over 380 retailers in this market. You can even find small souvenir stalls where they sell duplicate watches, etc. You can make a purchase in this entire market at a very reasonable price provided your bargaining skills are sharp enough!
Location: DeiraAl Ras – Dubai

4. Bur Dubai Souk-

Popularly known as textile souk or old souk, Bur Dubai Souk is known for its vibrant variety of textiles and fabrics. One can find excellent grade silk, satin, cashmere, velvet, and wool. You can also purchase made to fit garments. The colorful ambiance of this market makes it an Instagrammable place. One can easily shop around for Indian saris, Kashmiri pashminas, local traditional abayas, and Persian carpets. The souvenir shops are home to some fascinating gifts like “Aladdin shoes”, Dubai t-shirts, and Burj Khalifa souvenirs.
Location: Bur Dubai ( Al Souk Al Kabeer) Souk – Dubai

5. Mall of the Emirates-

This is truly an architectural marvel. With more than 900+ stores, this multi-level Mall is renowned for world-class hotels, restaurants. One can choose from over 630 brands of clothes and footwear. People shop here for Apple products, Chanel and Burberry couture, and shoes. The fashion shows that transpire now and then are completely stunning. Ski Dubai which is the first indoor ski resort and snow park of the Middle East is located here. Moreover, there are over 180 amusement machines for complete family entertainment. In November 2005, it was named the World's Leading New Shopping Mall at the World Travel Awards in London. In 2017, Forbes named Mall of the Emirates as one of the top five shopping malls in Dubai.
Location: Sheikh Zayed Rd - Al Barsha Barsha 1 – Dubai

6. Souk Madinat Jumeirah-

Located near Burj Al Arab, this place is home to various boutique shops and luxury brand stores. A confluence of the ancient and contemporary Arabian design is the highlight of Souk Madinat. One can shop for a wide variety of goods like local handicrafts, fashion jewelry, branded clothes, souvenirs, spices, etc. Around 25 restaurants are offering delectable drinks and International cuisines. The entire marketplace is decorated with ornate lanterns and carved wood, giving it the traditional Arabian look.
Location: Al Sufouh Rd - United Arab Emirates

7. Burjuman Centre

Shopping at this place would not burn a hike in your pocket. This well- designed complex has a lot of fashion stores and gourmet eateries. There’s an amazing toy store at Burjuman center. There is a large entertainment and dining area. Work out on the latest machines at the “Fitness First” gym. “Carrefour” supermarket and VOX cinema are the superb highlights of this place.
Location: Khalid Bin Al Waleed Road - Dubai

8. Al Fahidi Street-

This is an iconic market for all sorts of electronic gadgets. Whether you’re planning to buy your cell phone, laptop, camera, or home appliances like ovens, washing machines, refrigerators, etc. this place has it all at reasonable rates. Make sure you receive an international warranty card on your purchase. Apart from this, you can shop around for Indian clothing, jewelry, fancy accessories, home décor items, watches, etc. One has to beware of the fake items being sold, especially the fake watches. It is an enthralling experience to visit here at night when the neon lights sparkle up the entire atmosphere.
Location: Al Fahidi St, Bur Dubai Al Fahidi – Dubai

9. Ibn Battuta Mall-

This themed Mall is renowned for its magnificent aesthetics. Themed around travels of 14th century Moroccan explorers, this Mall has over 400 outlets. You'd get a chance to explore the cultural centers of various countries like Egypt, Persia, India, China, Andalusia, the places where the Traveller visited. People generally shop here for books, fashion accessories, clothes, and souvenirs. It houses a VOX center, bowling alley and you can relish your favorite Starbucks coffee here.
Location: Sheikh Zayed Road - Dubai

10. Karana Shopping Complex-

The open bazaar-like ambiance provides an authentic shopping experience. This is indeed an exciting place for night shopping. The complex has over 300 shops and is speed across various buildings. You can pick up fashionable clothes and accessories at negotiable prices. You can also find gold and silver shops and kiosks at Karana Shopping Complex. You would even love to shop for handbags, fragrances, sportswear, gifts, souvenirs, watches, and shoes here. If you visit on weekends, you can enjoy Indian entertainment here. Claw machines are a major highlight.
Location: Kuwait Street, Next to Al Attar Shopping Centre, Karama – Dubai

11. Dubai Outlet Mall-

With over 200 stores, this Mall offers top brands at highly discounted prices. You’ll get one of the most amazing options in clothes, lingerie, shoes, sportswear and sporting goods, perfumes and cosmetics, electrical goods, watches, sunglasses, and toys. Brands like Splash and H&M and outlets like Sun and Sand Sports, the Outlet, Priceless, Stadium, and Tommy Hilfiger are present in the Dubai outlet mall. To avail the best deal of the week, you plan a visit to the Bargain Mondays. There’s a host of services for its customers like wheelchair accessibility, first aid room, baby cart, etc. To add to your glee, this Mall offers free parking. Dubai Outlet Mall even operates a shuttle bus from various hotels in Deira, But Dubai and Jumeirah.
Location: Dubai Al-Ain Road (Route 66), Dubai Land – Dubai

12. Perfume Souk-

To get an unparalleled heavenly experience, visit Perfume Souk, and get immersed in some awesome fragrances. Scents are an intrinsic part of Arabian culture. Here you can get to learn about oud and bakhoor and many intricacies of this fragrant material. Arabian scents can be tested via incense and purchased as a stick, powder, crystal, rock, or wood element. You can ask for a fully personalized aroma to be created depending upon your skin type and personality.
Location: Sikkat al Khali - Dubai

13. Global Village--

This place offers an experience that combines cultures of 90 countries across the world in one place. It is the world's largest shopping, tourism, leisure, and entertainment project. There are different pavilions designed according to different countries where you can shop for items unique to that country and enjoy authentic food of that place. An ideal place to shop for Iraqi handmade items, UAE souvenirs, sweets, clothes, and footwear. The architecture of the Global village is breathtaking. There is a chance for you to enjoy games and make an interesting tour of the place. The entry ticket costs only AED 15. This place is open from November until April.
Location: Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, Dubai, UAE

14. Textile Souk:-

This is the best place to shop for fabric and textiles. From raw silk to cotton, you can get every kind of fabric on Earth at this one place. Fabric in every imaginable design and form can be easily found here. Whether you’re looking for embroidered textiles or fine prints, this is a one-stop place for all kinds of cloth. The vibrant and colorful atmosphere of the textiles Souk is a sight to behold.
Location: Bur Dubai neighborhood, Dubai

15. Dubai Festival City Mall-

This is the place where celebrations never end. Whenever you may enter, the festive vibe would take over you for sure. The neon lighting and great music never let the spirit down. The lake outside adds up to the festive atmosphere. You can find great boutiques, exclusive stores, and designer abaya shops here and shop for some fantastic clothes, footwear, and accessories. Munch at one of the many excellent restaurants and if you’re lucky enough you can get to enjoy some free Cinema and dances.
Location: Dubai Festival City Mall, Dubai

16. Mercato shopping mall-

It is a compact two-floor Mall, compared to the other gigantic ones in Dubai. But it offers a holistic range of products that you can buy from. This is a community Mall, to cater to the needs of the residents of Jumeirah, but tourists also love this place for the gorgeous Italian themed décor and the soothing ambiance. It’s indeed a perfect place for relaxed shopping without rushing. The best items to shop from here are fashion accessories, footwear, confectionery, and homeware. The major highlights of Mercato shopping mall are Spinneys supermarket, VOX cinemas, Virgin Megastore, and Fun City. The food court offers delectable Arabic and French cuisines.
Location: Jumeirah Beach Road - Dubai

17. Al Ghurair center-

There are over 380 stores in one of these most luxurious malls in Dubai. There’s a perfect collection of Swarovski diamonds, makeup products, perfumes, accessories, and handbags. Al Ghurair center houses some of the international brands like Sun & Sand Sports, Carrefour, Max, Paris Gallery, and E-max. There are over 70 food and drink outlets. Apart from shopping, you can watch a movie here at the Reel cinemas or you can even book a room here and experience a stay in one of the most resplendent places in Dubai.
Location: Al Rigga Road – Dubai

18. Dubai Flea Market-

It is the second-largest second-hand market of UAE. Shop here for some amazing gadgets, toys, books, and clothes. The hallmark of Dubai Flea Market is the large variety and cheap prices of goods. You can get hold of some unique household items, quirky party pieces, funky jewelry, and limited edition shirts. An open-air shopping plaza and family entertainment options are the highlights of this Market. The Dubai Flea Markets are held on the first Saturday of each month at Safa Park, on the first Friday of each month at Uptown Mirdif, and the second Saturday of each month at Ibn Battuta.

19. Spice Souk-

For all the culinary experts out there, Dubai has a very exciting place for you all. The spice Souk is where you can find every imaginable spice in the world. People come here to shop exclusively for spices, nuts, and oils. The aroma of cinnamon, turmeric, cumin, cloves, pepper, nutmeg, and dries fruits are spread throughout the marketplace. Fresh stocks of these goods arrive here from India, Pakistan, and Iran. Some shops specialize in saffron, the most expensive spice in the world. You can even find wonderful teas, some beautiful lamps, and bowls in this market. This is indeed a place where you can put your bargaining skills to test.
Location: Sikkat Al Khail Road Al Ras, Dubai - United Arab Emirates

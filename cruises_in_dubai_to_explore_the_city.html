Top 12 cruises in Dubai to explore the city

Your trip to Abu Dhabi is incomplete until you’ve experienced life on a luxury cruise. Here, we share with you the top 12 cruises in Dubai to explore the city.

1. Arabian Gulf and Emirates Voyage Cruise-

The ships are Azamara Pursuit and Azamara Journey. Till October 2018, the cruise sailed through the Mediterranean Sea but in January and February 2019 Azamara Quest started a visit to Southern Australia, New Zealand, and up to Cairns. There are a great number of options available from which you can select i.e. 205 Verandah, 44 Suites, 26 Interior Staterooms, or 71 Oceanview. Room service menu and 24 hours room service are available to all the staterooms. The food on this cruise is outstanding and you’ll fall in love with the vibrant ambiance. You can even enjoy a spectacular show put up by talented dancers and musicians. Duration: 7 nights
Ship: Azamara Quest
Departure & Return: Dubai
Cost: $ 3,512 approx
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

2.The spice route cruise-

The Spice Route Cruise also has Azamara Quest and other ships are Azamara Journey and Azamara Pursuit. You can select among various Suites, Interior Staterooms, Verandah, or Oceanview. The food here is delectable and you can even relish some wine. Also the fabulous artists, both dancers, and musicians put up an enthralling show. A trip with this cruise would really be an enjoyable one. Duration: 19 nights
Ship: Azamara Quest
Departure: Dubai
Return: Singapore
Cost: $ 7,118 approx
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

3.Jewels of Arabia and India cruise 7874-

Jewels of Arabia & India Cruise 7874 offer various ships such as Seabourn Encore, Seabourn Ovation, Seabourn Quest, Seabourn Sojourn, and Seabourn Odyssey. Till October 2018 it offered 7 to 21 nights tour but now the duration is 18 nights. Taste luxury at its best in the 5-star dining restaurant, which has a seating capacity of 380. All rooms are very spacious. There are fabulous amenities that will make your experience a pleasurable one. Grab a drink at the minibar. On the cruise, there’s also a flat-screen TV and a living area. Duration: 18 nights
Ship: Seabourn Encore
Departure: Dubai
Return: Singapore
Cost: Verandah: $ 9,299 approx, Suite: $ 23,499 approx
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

4. Indian Ocean & Orchid Isles Cruise 7874 A-

Indian Ocean & Orchid Isles Cruise 7874 A offers various ships such as Seabourn Encore, Seabourn Ovation, Seabourn Quest, Seabourn Sojourn, and Seabourn Odyssey. There are 4-5 star dining venues for you to relish some great delicacies. The various cooking shows, movies, small shows, motivating lectures, and other special shows organized on the cruise are a true delight to watch. Enjoy some fabulous beverages as the soothing piano music plays in the background. There are great options for wine, coffee, and even tea. Duration: 25 nights
Ship: Seabourn Encore
Departure: Dubai
Return: Denpasar (Bali)
Cost: $ 12,299
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

5. UAE Oman Bahrain Qatar cruise-

This Trip is done with the ship MSC Lirica and other ships are MSC Divina, MSC Armonia, MSC Opera, MSC Seaview, and MSC Orchestra, MSC Sinfonia, etc. You'll get the best of facilities for a comfortable and enjoyable journey. Most of the cabins have a fabulous sea view and very fewer quarters have a private balcony. But all the cabins have TV, AC, Wardrobe, refrigerator, bathroom, towels, etc. The private suites have minibar, sofa and other extra facilities. Duration: 11 nights
Ship: MSC Lirica
Departure: Dubai
Return: Dubai
Cost: Inside: $ 989, Overview: $ 1,389, Suite: $ 2,619
Star Ratings: 3 Stars

6. UAE Bahrain Qatar Oman cruise-

This Trip is done with the ship MSC Lirica and other ships are MSC Divina, MSC Armonia, MSC Opera, MSC Seaview, and MSC Orchestra, MSC Sinfonia, etc. You can enjoy all the facilities such as TV, AC, refrigerator, washrooms, towels, etc. that would make your journey really comfortable and cool. You can have an eye on some magnificent plays and shows at the theatrical presentation, which generally lasts for 30 minutes. Duration: 11 nights
Ship: MSC Lirica
Departure: Dubai
Return: Dubai
Cost: Inside: $ 989, Overview: $ 1,389, Suite: $ 2,619
Star Ratings: 3 Stars

7. Pearls Of Arabia & India cruise-

The trip is done with Seabourn Ovation. Pearls of Arabia & India Cruise 8867 offer various options in ships such as Seabourn Encore, Seabourn Quest, Seabourn Sojourn, and Seabourn Odyssey. This ship began its services from May 2018. You can enjoy all the facilities such as TV, AC, refrigerator, washrooms, towels, etc. Their hospitality is out of the world. Every day they provide fresh flowers and fruits to their tourists. This ship offers inclusive beverages and no gratuity policies. If you plan for a party on a Dubai cruise, go for it. There are some fabulous bars and lounges where relish wine and enjoy champagne. Duration: 18 nights
Ship: Seabourn Ovation
Departure: Dubai
Return: Singapore
Cost: Verandah: $ 8,998, Suite: $ 18,498
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

8. Arabian Peninsula, UAE Bahrain Qatar cruise-

The ship that is considered under this trip is MSC Splendida. This is one of the most magnificent cruises because of sunshine and various offshore activities such as shopping, desert safari, and a glimpse of the ancient Arabian cultures. 80% out of 1,637 cabins have the oceanic view and many of them have private balconies which are simply resplendent. Breakfast for all the cabins is provided complimentary from 7:30 to 10 am. Overall, a nice option to consider for a cruise tour of the city. Duration: 7 nights
Ship: Seabourn Ovation
Departure: Dubai
Return: Dubai
Cost: Oceanview: $ 1,259, Balcony: $ 1,349, Suite: $ 2,849
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

9. Indian Ocean Gala cruise-

The journey is continued with the most majestic ship Nautica. The other ships are Marina, Regatta, Sirena, etc. This cruise has a lot of opportunities for all its tourists. Not only do the visitors enjoy but take along a great learning experience. You can attend workshops on Microsoft Office, Digital photography; Internet, etc. If reading is your thing, the library on this cruise would surely be adored by you. Even the cabins are also very posh and comfortable. Duration: 30 nights
Ship: Nautica
Departure: Dubai
Return: Cape Town
Star Ratings: 4 Stars

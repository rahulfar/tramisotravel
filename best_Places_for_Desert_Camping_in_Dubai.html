10 Best Places for Desert Camping in Dubai

Dubai is a city of skyscrapers and architectural marvels. But, the natural beauty of the city is matchless. The desert camping in Abu Dhabi can be an enthralling experience with great adventurous sports for you to enjoy.

1. Al Dhafra Beach:-

It is one of the best beaches in Abu Dhabi and is indeed an ideal place for desert Camping. The beautiful sea in the desert is an Instagrammable spot. You can enjoy some thrilling water sports like diving, snorkeling, and kayaking and even go fishing, and then enjoy the grilled fish while enjoying the beautiful sight. Camping at the Al Dhafra is a heavenly experience.

2.Umm Al Quwain:

Camp near the shoreline and enjoy the spectacular scenic beauty at Umm Al Quwain. You can easily get the sight of the beautiful flamingos with the help of binoculars when you camp here. If you love to spend time in nature, this is a place for you. A perfect place to pitch a tent and spend a great time with friends and family. But camp a little far from the shore, else the high tide can flood your tent. You can visit the fishing villages along Al Raas, and catch a few for dinner!

3.Fujairah-

A great place for private overnight desert camping, Fujairah is one of the most sought- after camping places because of the convenience to pitch a tent here. It is one of the only Emirate that is located on the East coast of UAE. Go snorkeling with your travel mates and explore the magnificent marine life underneath. At Fujairah, you'd get a picturesque view of the mountains while you relax at the secluded beach.

4. Al Qudra Lakes-

Located near the Mall of the Emirates, this place is a must-visit for all tourists. There’s a lively atmosphere with a plethora of fun activities to engage in. You can go for sand dune bashing and camel riding. Camping here with family and friends would be an enthralling experience. You’d be mesmerized at the sight of the oasis in the wide desert. Get a glimpse of the swans and flamingos and capture them as they stroll near the waters.

5. Hajar Mountains-

It is never obsolete to camp on mountain ranges and Dubai offers you a great opportunity for a hilltop adventure. Spread evenly between Oman and UAE, Hajar mountains are the first love for all campers visiting Dubai. Before setting up your camp, you'd have to hike and trek to find a perfect place to pitch up a camp, which is really adventurous. Admire the spectacular views and explore Dubai wildlife on your way!

6. Jebel Al Jais-

Popularly known as “ Great Canyon of Oman”, Jebel Al Jais is the tallest mountain in UAE. The views of the mountain range are really breathtaking. The slope is comparatively flat and it is easy to climb the mountain and thus favorable for hiking. Camp here to enjoy the scenic views and starry nights amidst a peaceful environment.

7. Fossil Rock-

Fossil Rock, located in Sharjah is surrounded by sand dunes and is admired by adventure freaks visiting Dubai from all over the world. It's a perfect place for large groups to have fun amidst the vast stretch of desert. The thrill of dune bashing and off-roading in a 4×4 is indeed an unforgettable adventure at Fossil Rocks. You can even enjoy some water sports here to get a respite from the scorching heat of the desert. You can visit Big Red and the Camel Rock if you would like to try dune buggying at Fossil Rock.

8. White Sands Beach-

Situated between Fins and Wadi Shab, White Sands Beach is a beautiful place where you can enjoy the coastline. Camp on a magnificent cliff above the sandy beach and enjoy the great views while listening to the soothing sound of waves. You can amuse yourself by being engaged in some of the amazing watersports like paddle boarding, snorkeling, diving, and fishing in Emerald pools. It'll be a great delight to find sea urchins close to the beach.

9. Khasab and Al Sayah Plateau-

have a camping experience in Khasab and Al Sayah Plateau, you'd have to make some prior arrangements. It is situated in Musandam, and since it passes through the borders at Al Rams, you've got to apply for a 10-day tourist visa and also keep yourself updated on information about border rules and regulations applied here. The fees for the tourist visa is INR 1,000. But here you can enjoy some exciting sports like mountain biking, trekking, snorkeling, swimming, and diving. Or you can simply hang out at the camp, and enjoy the astonishing views.

10. Bedouin camp-

Away from the hustle and bustle of the city, Bedouin camp provided you the luxury desert camping experience, with all civic amenities. Here you’ll get a glimpse of the Bedouin customs and traditions and taste resplendence to its best. A perfect place to enjoy a classic barbecue dinner with family and friends. Enjoy the belly dance performances, which is indeed a delightful show. Camel riding and sandboarding are other exciting activities that you can engage in at the Bedouin camp.

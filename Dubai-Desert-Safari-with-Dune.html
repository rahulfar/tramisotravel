WHY SHOULD I GO FOR THIS?

  • Chance to experience the unspoiled Arabian Desert with a professional crew.
  • Experience the thrill of a lifetime as you go on an exhilarating 4X4 drive over the endless sand dunes.
  • Take in the magnificent desert sunset views.
  • Enjoy camel ride and other desert-exclusive activities like sandboarding, quad biking etc. (but available only upon request).
  • Cool down at the desert campsite.
  • Relax in its Bedouin-style tent, smoke Shisha and get your hands tattooed with beautiful Arabic henna designs.
  • Dress yourself up in traditional Emirati attire and pose for some great souvenir photographs.
  • Enjoy a lavish BBQ dinner with a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian foods.
  • Watch the spellbinding belly dance and the impressively dervish Tanura performance.

TOUR OVERVIEW

A desert safari is an escape from the city’s classy sights and chaotic ambience. It’s true but that doesn’t mean that you’ve to do it while skimping on luxury. Now don’t worry if you’re cost-conscious; our reasonably priced desert safari lets you experience the desert like a royal! Wonder what’s special about this desert adventure? Besides several exciting activities, this tour offers roundtrip transfers in the comfort of a high-end 4X4 vehicle.

Enjoy an exclusive or shared pick up in a well-maintained 4X4 transport. Once you reach here, your expert driver will take you on an extraordinary dune bash across the endlessly vast desert landscape. As part of this super adventurous ride, there will be a stop to stretch your feet, take in enthralling desert sunset views, and of course, click some great pictures too.

The second part of your desert safari will head you to our modest desert camp where nature, culture,and Arabian hospitality seamlessly come together. Get a deeper glimpse into the ancient Bedouin traditions with many activities such as camel ride, henna painting, and Shisha smoking. If you're up for more desert fun and adventure, add experiences like sandboarding and quad biking inside the circle only(charges apply).

After all exclusive desert experiences, park yourself to relish an appetizing BBQ dinner with vegetarian and non-vegetarian delights, together with a dose of genuine traditional entertainment like intricate Tanura performance and Belly Dance show. End your unforgettable desert expedition with a luxurious drive back to your pick-up spot.

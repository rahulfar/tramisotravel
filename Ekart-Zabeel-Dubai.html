WHY SHOULD I GO FOR THIS?

  • It gives you the chance to enjoy the UAE’s first, state-of-the-art indoor go-karting facility. 
  • It’s placed in the Zabeel Extension of the Dubai Mall which also hosts some of Dubai’s most popular attractions such as Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo, KidZania, Dubai Ice Rink and VR Park. 
  • Opportunity to ride the most advanced yet environmental friendly electric go-karts. 
  • It’s ideal for both expert racers and beginners (including first timers). Just make sure that you’re above 14 years and meet all height and weight criteria (check below). 
  • You can hit the well laid-out track lanes while racing against friends, family or colleagues. 
  • It’s extremely safe and covers comprehensive safety briefing by experts. 
  • All protective gear such as helmet and racing jump suit are included. 
  • It not only lets you track your lap times but also allow to share it online. 

TOUR OVERVIEW

Take a break from Dubai’s usual sights and head to Ekart Zabeel Dubai! It promises you the ultimate go-kart racing experience at the country’s first state-of-the-art indoor karting facility. Created by Emaar Entertainment, it’s located in the Zabeel Extension of the Dubai Mall which is not only the world’s biggest mall but also home to some of the region’s most visited attractions such as Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo, Dubai Ice Rink, KidZania, and VR Park.

The vast indoor space boasts of a fleet of 11 high-performance, electric go-karts that is environmental-friendly as well with zero emissions. Whether you’re a racing veteran who wants to experience the swiftest ever acceleration or a novice looking to put your racing skills to test, it caters to every ability. In fact, you don’t have to be an expert to try them; even beginners and first timers can make the most of this enormous track. You just need to be above 14 years, 140 centimetres tall and not weigh more than 120 kilograms.

As safety and track maintenance (of highest standards) are central to the Ekart racing, the first thing that awaits you here is a comprehensive safety briefing. You’ll be provided with all protective gear including helmet, racing suit etc. Once suited up, place yourself in the kart’s driver seat and off you go! Pick up your desired speed levels and zoom across the track lanes as you navigate the twists and compete against time or fellow racers. What’s more exciting is that your kart records your performance. This tracks your lap times and allows you to share it online too.

You’ll have the choice of a standard ticket or a double ticket with two timed sessions (ideal for die-hard racing enthusiasts). With exclusive race event packages for a minimum group of eight people, this is also a perfect venue for corporate events or friends / family day out. So gather your friends, colleagues or family members; a trip to Ekart Zabeel guarantees you an adrenaline-fueled, fun-filled outing.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • Passport or Emirates ID is mandatory to be carried during this activity.
  • Drivers must be at least 14 years old, 140 cm tall and weight less than 120 kg.
  • Wear enclosed footwear.
  • Make sure to wear racing suits over their clothes with lose clothing tucked inside.
  • Ensure face and/or head covers provided are worn under their helmet to maintain hygiene.
  • Notify management of any disability, intellectual or physical, which may affect their ability to drive.
  • Notify management of any pre-existing health conditions, including any physical limitations or medical conditions including but not limited to: neck weakness, back weakness, broken bones, heart conditions, nervous disorders, motion sickness, epilepsy,or if pregnant.
  • Participate in the safety briefing prior to driving.
  • Follow the instructions of the track marshals.
  • Drivers are not allowed to drive while under the influence of a substance such as alcohol, medication or drugs that may impair driving ability.
  • Any participant driving dangerously will be removed from the race and no refund will be made.
  • Drivers are responsible for, and agree to pay for any damage to karts and equipment.
  • Glass containers or bottles are not allowed on the circuit.
  • Food and drinks are not allowed once participants enter the turnstiles.
  • Failing to abide by the code of conduct and instructions will result in the removal of the guest and no refund will be provided.
  • There will be no refund for partly utilized services and in case if the guest doesn’t show up at the start time mentioned in the tour itinerary, the service provider reserves the right to charge 100% no-show fee.

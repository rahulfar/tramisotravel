20 most thrilling theme parks in Dubai

Dubai is not just a place to see or shop or dine. What makes it a family vacation hotspot is its exciting adventure and theme parks, that offer rides for toddlers as well as adults. To ensure that you don’t miss out on some of the most exciting places, we share with you the list of 20 most thrilling theme parks in Dubai:

1. Ferrari World Theme Park –

It is one of the most sought after theme park, loved alike by youngsters and adults. The world's first Ferrari branded theme park, this place is a dream destination for admirers of speedy rides. The cybernetic tour of the Ferrari factory is truly a wonderful experience. Taste the highest of speeds at the go-karting track. A ride on the Formula Rossa coaster is sure to thrill you to the core. Turbo track and Flying aces are some other exciting rides you can enjoy in this fantastic theme park.
Timings: 11:00 AM-8:00 PM
Location: Yas Leisure Drive, Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Price: AED 284/ INR 5500

2.Aquaventure Waterpark-

Spread across an area of 17 hectares, this waterpark is indeed one of the best in Abu Dhabi. Riding through the shark tunnels, going to the Splasher’s Island safari is some of the most exciting things to do here. For those who’d love to spend some peaceful time, 700 m stretch of pristine Beach awaits to welcome. For the thrill-seekers, Poseidon’s revenge, leap Of faith, and Aquaconda are sure to be enjoyed. Aquaventure has won the title of the “Best Waterpark in the Middle East 2015” on TripAdvisor and “Middle East’s Leading Waterpark 2016” by the World Travel Awards.
Timings: 10:00 AM-6:00 PM
Location: Crescent Road, Atlantis, The Palm, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: 100 AED/ 2000 INR

3.IMG Worlds of Adventure-

It is Dubai's first mega theme park and is a paradise for comic lovers. Maintained by the Ilyas and Mustafa Galadari Group, it's size is larger than 28 football fields. The theme park is divided into 4 adventure zones with one zone completely dedicated to exciting rides based on dinosaurs, yes you’ll get the Jurassic world experience in this zone. The roller coaster rides, haunted hotels, your favorite superheroes whether it be Iron Man or Spider-Man, await you for an extraordinary adventurous trip.
Timings: 11:00am-8:00pm (Sun-Wed)
               11:00am-10:00pm (Thurs-Sat).
Location: Sheikh Muhammad Bin Zayed Road, City of Arabia, Dubai, United Arab
EmiratesPrice: AED 200/ 3900 INR

4. Legoland Water Park-

The best thing about this water park is that it has something for everybody. There are numerous fun rides for your kids in the age group of 2 to 12 years. You can enjoy here with your entire family even if you have toddlers with you. Legoland has carefully designed rides for your toddler where he too can Splash and play and proper care has been taken of their safety and hygiene. One is sure to enjoy the exciting rides like Joker soaker, splash safari, wave pool, tube slides, family raft and you can even go about creating a LEGO raft.
Timings: 10 am to 7 pm
Location: Jebel Ali, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: 250 AED/ 4800 INR

5. Ski Dubai-

It is the world's largest indoor ski complex spread across an area of 22,500 sq meters. The temperature here stays around just -1 degrees to 2 degrees Celsius throughout the year. The entire ambiance is so welcoming and chilling that offers you a perfect respite from the scorching heat of the city. There’s a chance for you to do a crash course in skiing and learn from qualified instructors. Watching the snow March of the penguin is a sight to behold.
Timings: 10:00am-11:00pm (Sun-Thurs)
                9:00am-10:00pm (Fri)                 9:00 am-11:00 pm (Sat) Location: Sheikh Zayed Road, Interchange 4, Mall of the Emirates, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: AED 200/ 3900 INR

6. Bollywood Park-

This theme park is a must-visit for Bollywood lovers. Designed in the form of a shooting set, you can find here your favorite Bollywood stars in action roles. The 5 different zones of this Dubai theme park are Bollywood Boulevard, Mumbai Chowk, Rustic Ravine, Royal Plaza, and Bollywood Film and Studios. It would give you a glimpse of the film city with romance, stunts, and all those cinematic elements at display in every corner of this theme park. Watching the live performances whether it be the scenes or the dances, is a true delight for all visitors.
Timings: 1:00pm-10:00pm (Sat-Wed)                1:00pm-11:00pm (Thurs & Fri)
Location: Sheikh Zayed Road, E 11 Exit 5, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: AED 165/ INR 3,632

7. Wild Wadi Waterpark

It is the first park in the Middle east to have won the Dubai quality award gold in 2007. It is themed around the famous Arabian folklore character Juha. There are around 30 fantastic rides and you should not miss the Breakers bay, which is the largest wave pool in the Middle east. Jumeirah sceirah is another exciting ride this waterpark offers. There’s a store for swim and beachwear and also several kiosks in the complex. The friendly lifeguards make your visit a memorable one.
Timings: 10 am to 6 pm
Location: Jumeirah Road, Umm Suqeim 3, Dubai, UAE
Price: 232 AED/ 4500 INR

8. Dubai Global Village

It is one of the most fascinating places in Dubai. Here you can find the culture and cuisine of around 90 countries in just one place. This is an amazing family destination that provides a great opportunity to dine, shop, and entertain by immersing in the cultures of various countries. The open-air shows and performances are spectacular and you can have fun by playing some entertaining games. The festive ambiance here is one reason why people throng to this place, another being the reasonable costs of the tickets.
Timings: Saturday to Wednesday from 4:00 pm – 12:00 am
               Thursday, Friday and Public Holidays from 4:00 pm – 1:00 am                Mondays are dedicated to Families and Ladies only.
Location: Sheikh Muhammad Bin Zayed Road, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: Starting from AED 15/ INR 30

9. Motiongate Dubai-

-Themed after the 3 most popular motion picture studios of Hollywood- DreamWorks Animation, Lionsgate, and Columbia Pictures, this park is a fun place to be in. There is a great number of activities for the entire family that take you to the fantasy world of your favorite characters. How would it be to explore the world of Madagascar and the Kung Fu Panda? The multimedia theatre shows are enchanting. You can take part in the martial arts of the Furious Five show and the interactive play zone would thrill you to the core. Hey, don’t miss the battle of Ghostbusters!
Timings: 12:00pm-10:00pm (Thurs-Fri)
               12:00pm-9:00pm (Sat-Wed) Location: Sheikh Zayed Road, opposite to Palm Jebel Ali, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: 222 AED/ INR 4,300

10. Yas Waterworld-

This award-winning water park has a lot in store for you. With over 40 exciting rides, you can spend a Fun-filled day here with your family. It is themed around an original story of a brave girl named Dana. You can enjoy some of the most adventurous activities here like the Bubble’s barrel, Rush Rider and bandit bomber. Moreover, Pearl Diving Adventure and the interactive treasure hunt have been the most sought after rides of this place.
Timings: 10:00am-6:00pm
Location: Yas Leisure Drive, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Price: 210 AED/ 4000 INR

11. Warner Bros World-

It is the first indoor theme park in Dubai. Warner Bros Buffer zone would remind you of your favorite cartoon character. It won’t be a good idea to miss out on the 20 states of art recreation. Riding The Formula Rossa, the fastest roller coaster ride in the world would be an enthralling experience. Visit Gotham city with your family for a full-on entertaining day. Finally, it's a great place to shop around for your favorite brands.
Timings: 10:00am-8:00pm
Location: Al Falah Rd, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Price: AED 300/ INR 5, 826

12. Laguna Waterpark-

This waterpark is divided into 5 distinct zones and is a peaceful place to enjoy and relax. The La Mer Beachfront is what adds to the Fun day at Laguna. Your young ones can have a gala time chilling on some amazing rides like Acqua play rain forest or the Splash pad. To complete your family day, you can grab on some delicacies from the nearby restaurants.
Timings: 10:00 am -10: pm
Location: La Mer, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Price: Full day passes for AED 125/ 2400 INR, exclusive cabana experience for AED 1100/ 21300 INR

13. Dreamland Aqua Park-

Spread across an area of 2,50,000 sq km, Dreamland has some of the most fabulous rides like Twisting dragons and Aqua play. Enjoy here the go-kart circuits and the gigantic wave pool. The best part is that you can get overnight campaign facilities which are a really fun thing to do with friends and family. Added to this are the live concerts at the amphitheatre, varied electronic games, and simulators in the video zone. Then you can savor the delectable cuisines in the great restaurants.
Timing:10 am- 8 pm
Location: Umm Al Quwain, Dubai
Price: AED 105.

14. VR Park-

It is a virtual reality themed park spread across an area of 75,000 sq feet. There are 30 exhilarating rides for all age groups in the 2 storeyed building. The best games are Dubai drone( VR tour by drone over Burj Khalifa), Dune Bash ( trip across the desert of Dubai), and Paradrop VR. If you've never experienced it before, you're sure to get blown away by this astounding technology. It is indeed a tech marvel.
Timings:Sun- Thu- 10:00am- 12:00 midnight
                Fri- 9:00am-1:00am                 Sat- 9:00am-12:00 midnight Location: Dubai Mall, Financial Centre Road, Dubai
Price: AED 90.

15. Kidzania-

This kid’s zone is indeed a great way to make your child learn some practical skills. This place provides an opportunity for young ones to enter the world of adults. Through fun and gaming, they can learn some decision-making skills while acting in real-life scenarios. They'd get to earn and spend in a pseudo world of markets.
Timing: 9 am - 11 pm
Location: The Dubai Mall
Price: AED 182

16. Splash Pad-

This beach amusement can prove to be a perfect family vacation destination. With fantastic highlights like giant tipper bucket, water squirters and climbing frames provide for an imagination playground. Let your kids enjoy some high-quality play equipment while you relax at the beach. Seahorse cannons and superwave wheels are some adventurous sports. All in all, this is an ideal place to celebrate your child’s birthday or plan a family gets – together on a vacation.
Timing: 9 am - 8 pm
Location: BeachFront, North side of THE BEACH, Near Roxy Cinema, Dubai
Price: AED 99.

17. Hub Zero-

It is a truly fabulous amusement park with terrific games like 360 degrees battle in the Dragon Age, 3D Resident Evil Zombie Fight, Virtual Arena, etc. A perfect place for the most exciting family time as this park has something for everyone. A Fun day spent here would become one of the most memorable one in your life. You are sure to be spellbound by the hi-tech fun sports at Hub Zero.
Timings: 12:00pm-12:00am (Thurs-Fri)                12:00pm-11:00pm (Sat-Wed) Location: Al Madina Street, City Walk, Dubai 123311, United Arab Emirates
Price: AED 200/ INR 3,900

18. Aventura Parks-

This park provides an outdoor setting for all the adventure. Tree surfing, rope climbing, log balancing, and zip-lining are amongst the numerous thrilling activities that you get to experience here. Discover and Ranger circuits and the 25 m long Tarzan Jump would let you taste real adventure. The Ghaf forest Spread across the 5000 sq m provides a great basis for a natural Adventure. You're a true hero if you pass the 5 circuit levels successfully.
Timing: 11 am - 10 pm
Location: Mushrif Park, Dubai
Price: AED150.

19. Riverland Dubai-

This theme park takes you to a tour of the medieval times. The Riverland is divided into four zones: The French Village, Broadwalk, India Gate, and The Peninsula. The French Village takes you back to 16 th century Europe with the classical French music playing in the background and the signature olives adorning this zone. The boardwalk leads to Motiongate and you get to taste the America of the 1950s. India gate represents Bollywood twists and the southeastern cuisines lead you to the India of 1920s. A tour of 19th century England can be taken just by visiting The Peninsula zone. So, all in all, you'd get to traverse through history and this journey is surely going to be superb.
Timing: 10 am - 12 am
Location: Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai
Price: Starting from AED 175.

20. Wonderland Amusement Park-

This oldest park in UAE is internationally renowned. The family entertain zone has more than 100 rides. The kid’s zone called the Fort Fun is an excellent place for kids to thoroughly enjoy themselves, especially at the Rain Ball. The most amazing one is the 18 seater action ride where you get to experience water jet and the wind effects with the 4D system. And after all the tech fun, a camel ride comes as an icing on the cake.
Timing: 10 am - 12 am for Theme Park and 10 am to 8 pm for the Water Park
Location: Dubai - United Arab Emirates
Price: Starting from AED 40.

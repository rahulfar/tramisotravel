Water Parks in Dubai

Water parks are fun to visit and Dubai took its water parks to another level and has some of the best water parks in the world which are fun to visit. Also Dubai is a very high temperature country, because of that people love to visit water Park in Dubai both the tourists and the permanent residents of Dubai. Here are some of the best water Parks of Dubai:-

1. Aquaventure Water Park-

Aquaventure water Park is a huge water park with some zip lines, which are slides through shark-filled lagoons and a separate splash area for kids. The water park is located on the side of beach which gives the visitors a beautiful view. This could be termed as the best water park in Dubai according to the size and adventure experience of the water park. Their rating of the water park is 4.5 stars by more than 12,000 google users. For Special Attraction the water park has many restaurants in it, which offers number of delicious dishes and one can also book the hall in the water park for some function or party. You can walk in this water park without carrying anything because you get the swimming costume on the nearby shop which is authorized by the water park. The Timing and Days of the water park opens everyday at 10:00 AM and the closing timing depends on the current season. The Best time to book your tickets to this fun water park is in summers, because they have special discounts to offer in summers and also the closing time is extended in summer. The water park also offers some beautiful views to the visitors and it worth the time and money of the visitors.

2. Wild Wadi water park-

The Wild Wadi Water Park is an amazing and huge outdoor water park of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Located in the area of Jumeirah, near to the Burj Al Arab and the Jumeirah Beach Hotel, the water park is run by Jumeirah International, one of the Dubai-based hotelier. This is a fun water park to visit with the rating of 4.5 stars by more than 9,500 google users. There are some Special Attraction in the water park which are restaurants, which offers number of delicious dishes and also has a nice theme following the water park. You can walk in this water park without carrying anything because you get the swimming costume on the nearby shop which is authorized by the water park. The Timing and Days are from Sunday to Wednesday it is 10:00 AM to 7:00PM. On Friday it is 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On Saturday it is 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Eid Al Adha hours it is from 21 August to 25 August it is 10:00 AM to 10:00PM. The Best time to book one is in summers, because they have special discounts to offer in summers and also the closing time is extended in summer. This water park offers a exciting and adventures experience to the visitors and it is a worth visiting place.

3. Laguna Water park-

Laguna water park is shorefront water park with a large lazy river, beach cabanas, many food outlets, surf pool and slides. From three sides it is covered with ocean water and fun to visit. The ocean views are beautiful to watch and you can also sit and relax on the side of the water park and enjoy the beautiful view of the ocean. The rating of the water park is 4.1 stars by more than 400 google users. The Special Attraction of the water park are many food outlets in it, which offers number of delicious dishes and it is three sided ocean faced with some beautiful view to offer to the visitors. You can walk in this water park without carrying anything because you get the swimming costume on the nearby shop which is authorized by the water park. The Timing is 10:00 A.M. to 8:00 P.M weekend to weekend. The Best time to book one is in summers, because they have special discounts to offer in summers and also the closing time is extended in summer. This water park is best for people who need some beautiful ocean view with the fun and adventure rides of the water park.

4. Aqua Fun Water Park-

Aqua Fun Water Park is a water park in the sea which has many patterned air ballooned structure and is best place for kids. It will be a fun and adventures water park for kids. This water park is not build on land but in the actual ocean near the beach and one can also swim in the water as it is very same because it was build keeping kids in mind. The water park has the rating of 4.4 stars by more than 130 google users. The Special Attraction of the water park is an amazing and safe place for kids. You may carry a swimming costume for this different kind of water park. The Timing is 9:00 AM to 6:30 PM every day. The Best time to book one is not particular. You may book the ticket anytime because the prices are usually same. The water park is usually not crowded and only limited number of people are allowed in the water park at a particular point of time. This water park provides you a different experience, which is not offered by any other water park. Also the water park is more about adventure and different experience more than exciting and sky high water rides. It is a good place to visit for the people who have visited many water parks and wants something different.

5. Splash 'n' Party-

Splash ‘n’ Party is a good water park for kids in Dubai and has some different themes going on at different point of time. The water park is not in very large area and good for people who don’t have much time to spend and want their kids to have fun in the water park. The water park is more about kids and a fun place to visit for the kids. The rating of the water park is 4.3 stars by more than 900 google users. The Special Attraction of the water park is its themes for the kids and a fun and safe place to visit by the kids. You can walk in this water park without carrying anything because you get the swimming costume on the nearby shop which is authorized by the water park. The timing is 9:00 A.M to 8:00 P.M from weekend to weekend. The best time to book one is not specific as the price of the ticket is usually same and affordable.

Top 12 private beach and beach clubs in Dubai

If you look forward to an amazing holiday trip with your friends or family in Dubai, visiting a beach club or restaurant can be a great idea. You can have a great time relaxing, enjoying the panoramic views and the great cuisines. Here we've got for you a list of 12 private beach and beach clubs in Dubai:

1. Smoky Beach-

Located at the north of la Mer, this place can be a perfect spot to catch up with a great sunset view. One of the major highlights of Smoky Beach is the wide range of shisha that you can enjoy at the seaside. You're sure to enjoy the light snacks, the mocktails, hot drinks, and the salads and sandwiches. Moreover, the sushi rolls and all sweet desserts are their signature dishes. A perfect place to hang out with your buddies to get the chill vibes.

2.Shimmers:

Located on Madinat Jumeirah's private beach, this place is renowned for authentic Greek food. Moussaka and taramasalata are the most sought-after dishes. This Beach restaurant allows you to enjoy the Shisha flavors while relaxing in the sand. You can consider it for some casual family lunches. This Beach restaurant is indeed a place which you can choose for a romantic dinner.

3.Reem Al Bawadi-

Book a Cabana at the beach and enjoy the sea sight while savoring the delectable Lebanese and continental cuisines. If you’re a Shisha fan, this could be your place. There are plenty of options for vegetarian people. Since there is something for everybody, people throng to this place to add another beautiful feather to their cap of Dubai adventure. You’d get to enjoy live music to make your evening even more delightful, besides the cheerful and attentive staff.

4. Nammos-

This Mykonos Beach club is a great place for feasting in Abu Dhabi. Located at the Four Seasons Resort at Jumeirah beach, Nammos is one of the foremost Beach clubs that is a must-visit for all looking up to a resplendent rejuvenation. This place hosts A list of visitors like Lewis Hamilton and Leonardo di Caprio visit this Beach bar. Nammos offers you an unforgettable dining experience complete with great Shisha flavors to enjoy by the seaside.

5. White Beach-

The White Beach at Atlantis is one of the most sought after beach clubs. Guests love to Have Fun at the infinity pool. The open-air terrace restaurant offers a perfect view of the Dubai skyline as you enjoy the refreshing drinks. They have the Ladies Day on Monday when you enjoy unlimited beverages and grab a 50 percent off on a special food menu.

6. Zero Gravity-

Located at the Drop zone in Al Sufoun, next to Skydive Dubai, this is indeed a nice place for lunch at the beach or enjoying dinner with family. Zero Gravity is a Beach club, bar, restaurant, pool, and is a large scale event concept. Here’s the place where the party never ends. This Beach club can entertain around 5,500 guests and offers great amenities in a single package that they can purchase, like sunglass cleaning, cold towels, fresh fruit, and ice lollies. Don't forget to reserve for their free ladies’ night on Tuesday.

7. Drift Beach Club-

It is a private beach where you get to experience a luxurious lifestyle. The vibrant ambiance of the Drift beach club is really welcoming. The infinity pool adds to the party vibe. Enjoy the view of the Dubai skyline as you sit back and sip some great beverages. The dining experience at Drift Beach club is just amazing with delectable classical Provencal cuisines freshly prepared with choicest ingredients.

8. Barasti Beach bar-

At the Barasti, there’s a vast outdoor seating space where you get entertain yourself by the sea. Just swim, eat, and dance for the entire day or go for a soothing set back at the day bed as you enjoy the spectacular view of the Dubai Marine. The live music adds to the cheerful ambiance. If you do not wish to retire for the night, just go on and party out with your buddies. The great news is that entry to the Barasti Beach bar is absolutely free.

9. The Cove Beach-

A foodie's paradise, The Cove Beach is an all-day beach lounge. Set yourself free at the white sandy beach and give yourself a perfect treat. Relish the authentic Mediterranean – Riviera flavors and some other international cuisines, like those of Provence, Tuscan, south of France, or Italy. If you wish to enjoy seafood by the sea, this is the place for you. The finger-licking pasta would drive you mad and this place has great options for vegetarian tourists.

10. Andrea’s-

The aesthetic features of this posh Beach club are really mesmerizing. Situated at the Beachfront of Habtoor Grand Resort Autograph collection, this Beach club is one of the premier ones in Dubai. The elegant interior design, the Palm tree spattered terrace adorns the vibrant and cheerful ambiance. To get the festive vibe visit here on weekends and Mondays. The internationally inspired cuisine is the highlight of Andreea's where you get to relish handcrafted pizzas. The signature dishes are Mongolian style lamb pack, Paella Del Mar, and sushi.

11. Nikki Beach-

It is located at the Arabian Gulf on Pearl Jumeirah Island, this Beach club is indeed an architectural marvel. Spread across a vast area of 14,000 sq km, Nikki beach is the place where the party never ends. Lounge at the poolside and experience resplendence as never before. You can book your private cabanas or the three ultimate VIP cabanas which have their own pool, terrace, and cabana bed. There’s seating/lounging arrangement at the 4- tiered VIP area, from where you can get a great view of the entire Nikki beach complex.

12. Beach by Five-

This Beach spanning 150 m on West Palm is offered by Five Palm Jumeirah. The fabulous décor is a sight to behold. This Beach Club offers double width sun lounges, cabanas, and a glass-lined pool equipped with chandelier. At the deck, you get great dining and housing experience. The seafood is just out of the world and you are sure to love the fresh salads, mezze, and the sushi platter. The Mediterranean menu is a major highlight of this place. The arrays of hot and cold tapas from Spain, Greece, Turkey, and the Levant are inspired by a journey through the Mediterranean.

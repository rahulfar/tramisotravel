15 best coffee shops in Dubai

Cafes are great places for perfect snack time and when you're in Abu Dhabi this experience is extraordinary. Here we present to you 15 best coffee shops in Dubai.

1. Papparoti Café-

It’s a great place to rest and munch on some amazing snacks. You’d fall in love with the Bunuelos ( buns) served at Papparoti. There’s a great variety of fresh juices. Also, you'd get samples of uncooked tea to smell and choose your favorite taste. Don’t forget to try the Lotus Biscuits. The staff here is really welcoming and helpful, and so you get a great munching experience!

2.%Arabica-

It is a Japanese coffee house, renowned for high-quality coffee beans. Situated at the Dubai Mall, it was founded by Kenneth Shoji in Kyoto, Japan. The strong brand identity, the simple yet stylish ambiance at % Arabica makes it a must-visit for all coffee lovers. The goal of letting its customers “ see the world through coffee” and the amazing service you get here provides an enthralling experience that soothes your heart and mind.

3.MAKE Art café-

There’s an awesome rooftop ambiance and there’s overall great décor. The drinks served here are just fantastic. Located in the Al Fahidi neighborhood, this art studio cum café has a lot to offer to its guests. The ginger tea is really amazing and if you wish to try mojitos, don’t forget to order passion fruit mojito which is a signature item here.

4. Alrakwah Café-

Situated in Oud Metha, the Alrakwah Café is indeed a beautiful place to have coffee in Dubai. Relish great food made from the choicest ingredients. You can enjoy the tasty fruit cocktail and now the great news is that you can even have a Shisha party here. A great place to hang out with friends and spend a memorable evening with your loved ones. Also, the rates here are quite reasonable.

5. Third Avenue Boutique-

You have a great choice from the extensive menu at Third Avenue Boutique. Enjoy international, European, Deli, or grill, all of their preparations are outstanding. A great place to savor coffee, shakes, or some dessert. But the amazing balcony view is what lures tourists to this place. It is an eye-catching spot for Burj Khalifa dancing fountain, which can add a real charm to your evening.

6. Filli Café-

Try out some Filli snacks as you lookout for a perfect evening in Dubai. This place offers you some great Indian snacks like samosa chaat and even parathas. If you try their tea once, you're sure to cherish that taste for a lifetime. The Karak chai or the zaffron tea and maybe the saffron tea, all of them have a great aroma and are really refreshing drinks. Filli Café has a spacious ambiance with nicely laid out chairs and tables. There’s a convenience for both indoor and outdoor seating.

7. The Cereal Killer Café-

There’s great food presentation and the service is also just awesome. Best place to grab a sumptuous breakfast in Dubai. Try out hot chocolate, salted caramel, cheese nachos, Oreo milkshake, and cheese sandwiches, you’re sure to like them all. The interior is fantastic and offers you a cozy ambiance. Also, the staff is really helpful and friendly.

8. Caribou coffee-

Enjoy coffee prepared from authentic coffee beans at Caribou coffee in the Dubai Mall. It always keeps to the brand name offering quality brew in all its outlets. Relish any type of coffee you admire, and munch on some delectable snacks as you enjoy the splendid view of the Dubai skyline. The ambiance is cozy and it’s indeed a great place to be social. A perfect environment for a casual or formal meeting or for a great reading session for that matter. You can also have great family time at Caribou coffee.

9. Tiramisu Café LLC-

A cozy, light, and a nice place to relish great Italian cuisine. There's a fabulous outdoor seating arrangement for you to spend a great time with loved ones. Tiramisu café has reasonable prices for all its items and the overall ambiance is relaxing. Strawberry Banana Nutella is its signature item and you are served perfect cappuccino and espressos. This café keeps to its name as the classic tiramisu is really delicious.

10. Chainak Café-

Chainak Café is known for its superb preparations. Tikka paratha and cheese Mexican paratha is the most sought after dishes. Limca and pink lemonade is its signature drink. The quality is tea is top-notch especially the zafrani chai. There’s nice outdoor seating arrangement and people greatly enjoy Chainak fries and the lip-smacking molten choco cakes. And the icing on the cake is the courteous staff!

11. Café Cache-

The best thing about Café Cache is the reasonable prices which are really a surprise for visitors. Next, you're served the best of Lassi in Abu Dhabi and that too in a gorgeous ambiance. Even the juices and shakes are awesome and the mango blast is indeed a must-try. The quantity and quality that you get here have no match. This is the best place for sure to hang out with buddies and celebrate!

12. Roseleaf Café-

Situated amidst the Dubai Garden Centre, Roseleaf café is known for its unique ambiance. The greenery outside offers the perfect atmosphere for great snack time. Even the art- deco style interior décor is a sight to behold. There’s a lot to try out especially the chocolate peanut butter cake, choco caramel cake, brownies, the healthy granola bars, Pecan brittle, and the Tunisian cake. Fresh pittas, sandwiches, and pies are also great snacks to munch on in the Roseleaf café.

13. Pascal Clair sweets café and Bakery LLC-

It is the best place to pick up a good quality bakery and confectionery items at a decent price. The staff and management are purely professional and you can enjoy a perfect family evening at Pascal Clair. What you must try is fresh chocolates, macaroons, French pastry, and strawberry cheesecake. Relish some fantastic desserts, grab some amazing tarts and don’t miss out on beef pastrami in ciabatta bread.

14. Lessi House-

Well to have a perfect Indian snack time, Lessi House is the place for you. You’d get here the best fresh juices at competitive prices. It'll be a great idea to order ginger black tea with the Indian snack boonda. You can also savor hot chocolate here. Isn't it a delight to find falooda and Lessi all the way in UAE!

15. Dunkin UAE-

It is situated inside a petrol station and so you don’t have to worry about the parking spots. Their signature dish is donuts and you can almost all possible varieties of donuts having great flavors at Dunkin. There are amazing attractions for kids and the overall ambiance is really welcoming. They provide you excellent service and you get great offers. It's open 24 hours and is surely a nice place to relax and have a quick bite on your way.

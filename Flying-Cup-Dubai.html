WHY SHOULD I GO FOR THIS?

  • This is a unique culinary adventure which takes your dining experience in Dubai to exhilarating heights. 
  • This gives you the once-in-the-lifetime chance to dine in the clouds. 
  • Once you settle yourself in the pedestal platform and strapped into your seat, you’ll be gently suspended to a height of about 40 meters from the ground. 
  • Besides 20 plus seats for diners, there will be a five-member crew onboard to cater to your needs. 
  • Conducted at one of Dubai’s most stylish spots at The Beach, JBR, it takes your breath you away with the most astonishing views of Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah and the latest Bluewater Islands. 
  • As the table rotates, you’ll get to catch 360 degree views of some of Dubai’s most impressive sights such as Burj Al Arab and Ain Dubai. 
  • It is complemented by the most advanced safety features means this dining experience is as safe as much as it’s thrilling and fun-filled. 

TOUR OVERVIEW

Bored of all regular dining options in Dubai? You’ll then definitely want to check out the city’s brand-new dining venue with our Flying Cup Dubai tickets. It is absolutely far from your usual dining experience and offers a sky-high setting. Yes, this lets you dine in the clouds but that’s not all! It opens up for you the refreshing ever perspective to soak in the unprecedented views over modern Dubai from a bird’s eye view.

As you arrive at the spot in The Beach, JBR, get comprehensively briefed about the experience. Next, step on to the pedestal dining platform and make yourself comfortable in your seat which is attached to the table. Being part of the Belgium-based franchise which has an immaculate track record of over a decade, one thing striking about this dining concept is its advanced safety system wherein the seat belt will firmly secure your body in place. Once you’re well strapped in and ready, the table will be hoisted 40 meters off the ground with the help of an enormous crane.

For the next one and a half hours, you’ll recline back in your super comfy seats and skim over the city as the table gently rotates and gives you the unobstructed 360 degree views of Burj Al Arab, Ain Dubai, and practically the entire of Bluewater Islands, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marina etc. With a maximum of 22 diners plus a 5-member expert staff onboard, this further offers an intimate, out-of-the-world setting to savor the finest meals, snacks and drinks while taking in the cool fresh breeze.

efore you set out for Dinner in the Sky Dubai, it’s always recommended to leave your belongings in your designated locker. That said, we would definitely insist you to carry your smart phone / camera to capture the best ever selfies or images of this once-in-a-lifetime aerial experience which ensures a perfect balance of amusement, thrill and sightseeing.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • The total seating capacity for Flying Cup is 16 per trip.
  • You have to be at least 4 years old and above or at least 120 cm tall to be able to take part in Flying Cup experience. Person under 120 cm and above 105 cm may participate if accompanied by an adult.
  • An individual should be at least 18 years of age, who has full passive and active legal capacity for using the System.
  • Guests are advised to dress according to the weather. Please be advised that at 40 meter height the wind can be stronger and the temperature can be lower than usual. Once the platform has already been lifted, Flying Cup will not lower the platform for you if you request to change your attire, shoes, etc.

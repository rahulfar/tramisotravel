Day 1

ARRIVE AT ABU DHABI, WARNERS BRO


Arrive at Abu Dhabi Airport, Our Respresentative will greet you and Takes you to the Hotel, Check - in At Hotel, Take Proper Rest and after that Proceed for the Warners Bros.


Situated adjacent to the iconic Ferrari World Theme Park and the exceptional Yas Water World, Warner Bros World Abu Dhabi is a refreshing addition to Yas Island’s ever-dynamic assortment of leisure and entertainment attractions. Due to open in 2018, this indoor theme park is sure to spur exciting fun and thrill in an impressive environment. Interestingly, each of its offerings is inspired by classic characters that constitute the extensive library of legendary Warner Bros franchises, including Harry Potter, Hanna Barbera, Tom and Jerry, Looney Tunes, and Tom and Jerry.


Speaking of its main attractions, what we’ve now come to know is that there will be a total of 29 amazing rides ideal for all age groups plus a multitude of retail outlets and dining options – all set in a 1.65 square feet facility. Along with the rides and entertainment shows based on the DC Comics superheroes and the popular cartoon characters like Superman, Bugs Bunny, Scooby Doo etc, it will also introduce the world’s first Warner Bros branded hotel, adding to the collection of seven other exquisite hotels on Yas Island.


Built at a cost of $1 billion, this theme park is developed by the government-owned Miral which has been behind some of Abu Dhabi’s most futuristic attractions , such as Ferrari World, Yas Marina and even the upcoming Sea World as well as CLYMB. In fact, once it’s opened, Warner Bros World is expected to boost the number of visitors to Yas Island from 25 million to 48 million by 2022.


Overnight Stay at Hotel.


One of the world’s biggest, fully air-conditioned indoor theme parks, Warner Bros. World™ Abu Dhabi brings together all legendary characters from the studio under one roof. From Superman to Batman and Wonder Woman to beloved cartoon stars like Bugs Bunny,... Scooby-Doo, and Fred Flintstone, all and more are housed within the 1.65 million sq. ft. theme park with 29 state-of-the-art rides, interactive family-friendly attractions, unique live entertainment, multiple dining options, and exclusive shopping outlets. The magical experience starts as soon as visitors step into the Warner Bros. shield and are transported to different worlds of action, adventure and eccentricity through the park’s six immersive lands. Day 2

ABU DHABI - FERRARI WORLD


After Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed for the Tour to Ferrari World.

The world’s first Ferrari-branded theme park is home to a winning mix of Ferrari inspired rides and attractions, the biggest Ferrari Store and a range of authentic Italian dining options for the whole family. Beneath its iconic red roof, the entertainment destination houses 37 record-breaking thrill rides and an array of world-class cuisines.

The star attractions at Ferrari World Abu Dhabi include the new Flying Aces ride featuring the highest rollercoaster loop in the world, Formula Rossa which is the world’ s fastest roller coaster with a top speed of 250kmph in 4.9 seconds, family-friendly attractions, state-of-the-art simulators, electric-powered go-karts, live shows and an inspiring collection of racing memorabilia. Open 365 days a year, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi offers a unique experience for visitors of every age in a refreshing, indoor, climate-controlled environment.


Overnight Stay at Hotel.


Ferrari World Abu Dhabi is an amusement park where the world's fastest roller coaster, “Formula Rossa” is located. It is the first Ferrari -branded theme park and has the record for the largest space frame structure ever built. The Ferrari World Park took three years to develop ...and it officially opened to the public on 4 November 2010. It covers an area of 86,000 square metres. Open 365 days a year, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi houses numerous high-adrenaline rides featuring roller coasters, thrill rides, family rides, children's rides and shows also. Attractions also include two stores, racing events, concerts, luxury hotels and a selection of Itallian restaurants, makes it the top leisure and entertainment destination. This was named the "Middle East's Leading Tourist Attraction" in 2015 and "Middle East's Leading Theme Park" in 2017 at the World Travel Awards.

Day 3

After Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed for the Yas Waterworld,

This futuristic waterpark spans across an area as big as 15 football pitches, with 43 rides, slides, and attractions – five of which are one-of-a-kind. Visitors at Yas Waterworld can experience the world’s first and largest hydro-magnetic-powered, six-person tornado waterslide that extends up to 235 meters in length. For adrenaline junkies, there are three-meter-high waves on Bubble’s Barrel, which has the world’s largest surf sheet wave for flow-boards and body-boards. The Bandit Bomber, a 550– meter coaster, is the first with onboard water and laser effects. Riders can shoot jets of water at targets, drop water bombs and trigger special effects, while people below can spray them with water as they pass.

Sitting atop Jebel Dana, the towering core of the park’s altitude-defying summit and measuring eight meters in diameter, the park’s gigantic pearl is Yas Island’s newest landmark. The park draws on Abu Dhabi’s heritage with “the lost pearl” theme that follows the story of a young Emirati girl on a quest to find a legendary pearl as they make their way around the attractions, which include a souk and pearl-diving exhibits.

Overnight Stay at Hotel.


Yas Waterworld is a themed water park famous by its heritage and fantasy design. Its theme is based on an original story about a brave girl named Dana, who went on a journey to retrieve a lost PEARL. The theme is obvious in the parks' architecture with its pearl seen from outside the waterpark. All the characters, shops and restaurants are also based on the story. The Yas water park is one of the world’s best water park with a variety of attractions grouped into 4 categories: Adrenaline Rush, Exciting Adventures, Moving & Grooving and Young Fun. Since 2013, this waterpark has played host to the World Flowborading Championships. It also offers special training sessions to learn to ride the waves on body boards called Yas Flow League.

Day 5

HALF DAY DUBAI CITY TOUR + DESERT SAFARI TOUR WITH BBQ DINNER AND BELLY DANCING


Enjoy a yummy breakfast at the hotel. In the afternoon, enjoy Desert Safari with sand dunes and BBQ dinner. Pick-up from the hotel and take a thrilling ride through the deserts. Relish authentic Arabic cuisine. Be spellbound by the enthralling performances of exotic dancers. Watch the stars blinking at you in the wildness of the desert. Trip to the desert is full of fun and adventure. Experience the thrill of a roller coaster ride on the sand dunes. Take a pause and view the splendor of the beautiful sunset as it begins to lower on the sandy horizon. Continue the journey to the campsite in the heart of the desert. Upon arrival refresh yourself with soft drinks, tea or coffee. Freshly ground Arabic gawa (coffee ) is served while you enter the camp. Relax and recline in the tents with carpet and pillows while waiting for the sumptuous BBQ dinner to be served. Before we conclude the night, a belly dancer performs an enticing show to the rhythm of an Arabic music. Later return to the hotel and spend your night at the hotel.

Overnight stay at Hotel.


Dubai is a wide playground for adventure enthusiasts. Striking the desert sands in a monstrous 4x4 SUV is where the enjoyement begins. A Dubai desert safari is the ultimate travel experience complete with dune bashing, camel rides and a mouth-watering BBQ dinner accompanied by a belly dancing performance. Admire the spectacular scenery of the Red ... Dunes of Al Hibab and spend an adventurous 30 minutes of hard dune bashing. Later reach the camp where one should experience a short camel trek, try out the traditional henna hand decoration of the East, sand ski and enjoy a show consisting of belly dancing, and amazing Tanoura dancing. Other activities at the camp include quad biking, hookah, a sand art display, Arabic coffee, falcon photography, and a carpet stall. Dont forget to get a camera when going to a desert safari in Dubai as the views are breath taking and awesome.

Day 6

BURJ KHALIFA + MIRACLE GARDEN AND GLOBAL VILLAGE TOUR


After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed for the World's Tallest and the Most Attracted Building "The Burj-Khalifa".


No Dubai tour package from India is complete without visiting the pride of modern of day Dubai – Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world. this building is so tall that it can be seen from any part of the city. It has a total of 160 storeys and is of 829.8 metres high! Visiting “At the Top” inside Burj Khalifa is a popular tourist activity where you can enjoy a 360 degree view of this dazzling city from the 124th floor. Ticket prices vary as per the levels from general tickets to luxury tickets where you will also be served delicious refreshments which you can enjoy while being awestruck from the view.


And Later, Proceed for the Miracle Garden - Seeing the garden in full bloom with its 150 million flowers arranged in colourful arches and patterns, and the myriad shapes they form, is truly magnificent. Dubai Miracle Garden’s breathtaking landscaping has earned two Guinness World Records for the largest vertical garden in 2013 and world’s largest floral sculpture forming the shape of an Airbus A380 in 2016. We received the accolade for the Tallest Topiary Sculpture on 25 Feb 2018. The 18 metre sculpture feature is Disney’s first character floral display in the Middle East and is made from almost 100,000 plants and flowers, and weighs almost 35tons.


Then Proceed for the Global Village - It combines cultures of 90 countries across the world at one place. It claims to be the world's largest tourism, leisure, shopping and entertainment project. It is the region's first cultural, entertainment, family and shopping destination.

Overnight stay at Hotel.


Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world with height of 828 meters. It is the most popular among tourists attraction in Dubai, initially named as Burj Dubai. One of the building’s biggest attractions is its observatory deck. It is located on the 124th level of the building. There are 163 floors with 58 functional elevators, 2957 parking spaces, 304 hotels and 900 ... apartments. The building was completed in 2010. The total cost of building Burj Khalifa was 1.5 billion. The design of the tower is inspired by the shape of Hymenocallis flower. Burj Khalifa has broken two impressive records: “tallest structure” and “tallest free-standing structure” other than the world’s “tallest building”. The tower also holds world records for ‘highest occupied floor’, ‘highest number of stories’, ‘longest travel distance elevators’, ‘tallest service elevator’ and ‘second highest outdoor observatory deck’

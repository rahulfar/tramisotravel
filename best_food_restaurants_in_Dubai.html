10 best food restaurants in Dubai

Dubai is a place that guarantees a perfect vacation. And any trip is incomplete without savoring the best of cuisine the place has to offer. Here we present to you the 10 best food restaurants in Dubai:

1. A Cappella –

Located at the Pointe in Palm Jumeirah, A Cappella is one of the finest restaurants in Dubai. The food menu is designed as a passport and one has a variety of cuisines to choose from. Dishes from all across the world can be relished here whether it be Mexico, Italy, USA, India, China. One would love the amazing cocktails especially the Sour Pour made of yuzu syrup and orange bitters. Not just the food and cocktails but the shisha, the astounding view of the Atlantis and the dancing fountains add to the most beautiful evening in Dubai.
Opening Hours – 4:00 pm – 12:00 am
Price – AED 275 for two
Address – FB-08, East Marina, The Pointe Palm Jumeirah, Dubai, UAE
Website – www.acappelladxb.com
Contact Number - +971589719542

2. Sufra Restaurant -

It is one of the most amazing restaurants in Abu Dhabi offering Arabic, Indian and Western cuisines. It is known for the steak, cheesecake, salad, shawarma, and mocktails. You'd love this place for the brilliant hospitality and the enchanting décor. The view of the Creek Park from this place is enthralling. The large windows of the restaurant give an experience of dining in an urban garden.
Opening Hours – 6:30 am – 11:00 am, 12:30 pm – 3:00 pm, 7:00 pm – 11:00 pm
Price – AED 275 for two
Address – 20B St Hyatt Regency, Dubai Creek Heights, Dubai, UAE
Website – www.dubaicreek.regency.hyatt.com
Contact Number - +97145531212

3. Doors Freestyle Grill -

Known for the Middle Eastern and Mediterranean food, this restaurant is a perfect place for dining, business meets, or to have a perfect romantic evening. Beef butter fall steak is the best serving of this place known for its seafood. This steakhouse is an ideal place for a barbecue dinner with friends. One can enjoy the shisha at the terrace with the great views of the waters. A fire show is usually put up to cheer up the guests.
Opening Hours – 2:00 pm – 12:00 am
Price – AED 625 for two
Address – 8/5 – 314 AI Self St, Dubai
Website – www.doorsdubai.com
Contact Number - +971507000375

4. Danial Restaurant -

It is one of the most reasonable places to have an authentic Arabian feast in Dubai. It is also known for the Middle Eastern and Persian cuisines. This place offers one of the best spreads for a buffet in Dubai. There’s an open buffet for just 65 dirhams per person. The Danial Restaurant has in place menus for vegetarians, seafood lovers and meat lovers which makes this restaurant a perfect place for every kind of tourist.
Opening Hours – 12:00 pm – 12:45 am
Price – AED 150 for two
Address – Twin Towers, Rolex Building, 3 rd floor, Baniyas Road, Dubai
Website – www.danialrestaurant.com
Contact Number - +97142277669

5. Khyber -

It is a luxurious, elegant, and thrilling place, that has a lot to offer to its guests. The best thing about this place is that it has got excellent vegetarian options. The Indian cuisine presented here is one of the best in Abu Dhabi. But for the chicken lovers, the Afghan chicken curry is a delectable treat. What attracts people to this place is the unique cooking style and the fine ingredients that add a fantastic flavor to food.
Opening Hours – 5:00 pm – 11:00 pm
Price – AED 300 for two
Address – Palm Jumeirah Dukes Dubai Oceana, Dubai
Website – www.dukesdubai.com
Contact Number - +97144551111

6. BLVD on One -

A place known for the wide variety of options especially when it comes to European and central European cuisine. The hospitable staff at the restaurant makes your visit a delightful one. People love the buffet here which has numerous options to choose from. One can relish Mexican, Arabian, French, and even Thai cuisines available in the buffet. The mouth-watering desserts are a specialty at BLVD on one and desert cobbler is the most loved dish of this place. A spot-on combination of music and lighting makes your dining experience a memorable one.
Opening Hours – 6:30 am – 11:00 am, 12:30 pm – 6:00 pm, 6:30 pm – 11:00 pm
Price – AED 380 for two
Address – No 1, Palm Jumeirah FIVE Palm Jumeirah, Dubai
Website – www.hotelsandresorts.com
Contact Number - +97144559989

7. Al Dawar Revolving Restaurant -

The bright ambiance of this place makes it ideal for all sorts of celebrations. The only revolving restaurant in UAE, Al Dawaar is renowned for the Mediterranean food. One gets a lot of options in International and middle eastern cuisine. An amazing place to relish Sushi. Turkey, king crab, and lobster tail are the most sought after dishes. It's a great idea to venture to this place for a romantic dinner.
Opening Hours – 12:30 pm – 3:30 pm, 7:00 pm – 11:30 pm
Price – AED 400 for two
Address – 25 th floor, Hyatt Regency Dubai Hotel Al Khaleej Road, Deira Corniche, Dubai
Website – www.hyattresyaurants.com
Contact Number - +97142096911

8. Awtar Restaurant -

The best Lebanese restaurant in Abu Dhabi, Awtar restaurant is known for the Lebanese Mixed Grill which is the most delectable preparation. The live band adds to the cheerful ambiance of this place and it’s a delight to watch belly dancing. The Awtar restaurant offers a true display of Hyatt Brand and standards so this place is a must-visit for any tourist.
Opening Hours – 7:00 pm – 3:00 am
Price – AED 290 for two
Address – Sheikh Rashid Road, Grand Hyatt Dubai, Umm Hurair, Dubai
Website – www.hyattrestaurants.com
Contact Number - +97143172221

9. Mir Amin -

One of the finest places that deliver an excellent dining experience, Mir Amin is famous for chicken shawarma, zaatar, Tabbouleh, and Fattoush. One has great options in both Lebanese and Mediterranean cuisines. One of the most loved beverage is Moroccan green tea. But no alcohol is served at Mir Amin.
Opening Hours – 7:00 am – 2:00 am
Price – AED 235 for two
Address – Ribbon mall, unit 8, R4, building 1 Mir Amin motor city, Dubai
Website – www.miraminrestaurant.com
Contact Number - +97144227400

10. Yalumba -

It is a must-visit for all foodies. The warm and organic décor makes you feel so relaxed and cozy. It is renowned for the award-winning Friday brunch called Brunchology, which is a combo of sumptuous food and great entertainment. Yalumba is also known for its buffet in which more than 200 items are offered. The live cooking stations for Manakish and wraps are a true delight for all visitors.
Opening Hours – 5:30 am – 10:30 am, 12:30 pm – 3:00 pm, 7:00 pm – 11:00 pm
Price – AED 280 for two
Address – 51 St Airport Road, Dubai
Website – www.yalumbadubai.com
Contact Number - +97147022455
